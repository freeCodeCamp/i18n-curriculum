# --explanation--

`Wouldn't it be simpler...?` hutumika mara nyingi unapopendekeza suluhisho linaloonekana wazi au la mantiki. Kwa mfano:

- **John:** `Let's schedule three different meetings for each topic.`

- **Sophie:** `Wouldn't it be simpler to have one meeting and cover everything?` - Mtu huyu anapendekeza kuunganisha mikutano kuwa moja ili kuokoa muda.

Katika mazungumzo haya, Anna anaamini kwamba kumruhusu mtu kuamua jinsi ya kutumia ruzuku yake ni njia rahisi na bora zaidi.
