# --description--

`Is there` 和 `are there` 用于疑问句开头，询问某物的存在或出现。

- `Is there` 用于单数名词，例如 `Is there a solution?`

- `Are there` 用于复数名词，例如 `Are there any questions?`

# --questions--

## --text--

使用 `there is` 还是 `there are` 来询问某物是否存在，哪种方式是正确的？

## --answers--

`Is there a meeting scheduled for today?`

---

`Are there the new designer in the office?`

### --feedback--

记住单数使用 `is there`，复数使用 `are there`。正确的形式应该是 `Is there a new designer in the office?`

---

`Is there any chairs in the meeting room?`

### --feedback--

`Chairs` 是复数，所以正确的形式是 `Are there any chairs in the meeting room?`

---

`Are there a new designer in the office?`

### --feedback--

这个问题关于一个人，所以应该使用 `is there` 而不是 `are there`。正确的形式是 `Is there a new designer in the office?`
