# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia neno `near` kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`The developer near fixed the bug this morning.`

### --feedback--

`Near` haipaswi kutumika kabla ya kitenzi kwa njia hii.

---

`He near completed the project.`

### --feedback--

Hii si muundo sahihi. Tumia `almost` au neno lingine, si `near`, katika muktadha huu.

---

`The printer is near the manager's office.`

---

`She is near finishing the user guide.`

### --feedback--

Usemi unapaswa kuwa `almost finished` au `close to finishing`, si `near finishing`.

## --video-solution--

3

# --explanation--

`Near` hutumika kuelezea ukaribu wa kimwili au umbali mfupi kati ya vitu au maeneo.

Katika sentensi sahihi, inaonyesha wazi nafasi ya chapisho kuhusiana na ofisi ya meneja. Hii ni matumizi yaliyokusudiwa na sahihi ya `near` kama kiambishi mahali kuelezea eneo.
