# --description--

Acha $f_5(n)$ iwe nambari kamili kubwa zaidi $x$ ambayo $5^x$ hugawanya $n$.

Kwa mfano, $f_5(625\\,000) = 7$.

Acha $T_5(n)$ iwe idadi ya nambari kamili $i$ zinazokidhi $f_5((2 \times i - 1)!) &lt; 2 \times f_5(i!)$ na $1 ≤ i ≤ n$.

Inaweza kuthibitishwa kuwa $T_5({10}^3) = 68$ na $T_5({10}^9) = 2\\,408\\,210$.

Tafuta $T_5({10}^{18})$.
