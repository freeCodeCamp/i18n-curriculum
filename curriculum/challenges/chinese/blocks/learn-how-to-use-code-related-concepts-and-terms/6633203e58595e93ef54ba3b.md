## --text--

哪个句子正确使用了 `what`？

## --answers--

`She knows what does he like.`

### --feedback--

正确的结构应该是 `She knows what he likes`，从句中不应有辅助动词 `does`。

---

`What she is watching is very interesting?`

### --feedback--

这个语句错误地以问号结尾，因为它不是一个问题而是一个语句。

---

`I don't understand what you mean.`

---

`Tell me what is your name.`

### --feedback--

正确的问题形式是 `What is your name?` 或 `Tell me what your name is.`
