# --description--

Usemi wa kihesabu kama `do you know`, `could you tell me`, na `do you have any idea` hutumika sana kwa Kiingereza kuuliza au kuthibitisha taarifa kwa upole. Unavitumia vikitangulizwa na swali na muundo wa swali hubadilika kuwa sentensi chanya.

Mifano:

Swali la asili `What time is it?` limebadilishwa kuwa `Do you know what time it is?`

Swali la asili `How long does it take to read this book?` limebadilishwa kuwa `Could you tell me how long it takes to read this book?`

# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani kati ya zifuatazo inayotumia muundo `do you know` ikifuatiwa na sentensi chanya kwa usahihi?
