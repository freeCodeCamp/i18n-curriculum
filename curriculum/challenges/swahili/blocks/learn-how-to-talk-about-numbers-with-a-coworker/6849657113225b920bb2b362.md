### --feedback--

Maneno haya manne yanaonyesha muda gani kitu kinatumika, kwa kufuata muundo: nambari + kipimo + `per` + kipindi cha wakati.

# --explanation--

Muundo wa "nambari + kipimo + `per` + kipindi cha wakati" hutumika sana kuelezea muda unaotumika kwenye shughuli fulani. Kwa mfano:

- `The backup runs 30 minutes per day.` - Mchakato wa nakala rudufu huchukua dakika 30 kila siku.

- `The system runs for 20 hours per week.` - Mfumo hufanya kazi masaa 20 kila wiki.

Tom anasema jukwaa linatumika takriban `60 hours per week`, ikimaanisha timu hutumia muda huo kila wiki likitumia jukwaa hilo.
