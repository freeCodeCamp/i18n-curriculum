# --explanation--

Bob anaomba Sarah atoe taarifa kuhusu mara ngapi timu hujadili.

Tambua kwamba swali `How often does the team currently discuss back-end development?` hubadilisha mpangilio wa maneno linapokuwa kitu cha swali jingine. Mhusika na kitenzi hubadilishwa kutoka muundo wa swali wa awali.

Kwa maneno mengine, unapotumia swali kama kitu cha swali jingine, hubadilisha mpangilio wa maneno kuwa muundo wa kauli thabiti. Kwa mfano:

- `How often does he travel for work?` hubadilika kuwa `Can you tell me how often he travels for work?` - Swali sasa ni `Can you tell me...?`

- `What time does the meeting start?` hubadilika kuwa `Do you know what time the meeting starts?` - Swali sasa ni `Do you know...?`

Katika matukio yote mawili, kitenzi kiambatisho `does` kinatolewa na mhusika na kitenzi hubadilishwa kuwa muundo wa kauli thabiti linapotumika kama kitu cha swali jingine.
