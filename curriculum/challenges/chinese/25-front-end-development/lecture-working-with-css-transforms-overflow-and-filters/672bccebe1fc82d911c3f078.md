## --text--

How would you combine a blur effect of 5 pixels with a sepia effect of 50%?

## --answers--

`filter: blur(5px) + sepia(50%);`

### --feedback--

Remember how multiple filter functions are combined in a single filter property.

---

`filter: blur(5px), sepia(50%);`
