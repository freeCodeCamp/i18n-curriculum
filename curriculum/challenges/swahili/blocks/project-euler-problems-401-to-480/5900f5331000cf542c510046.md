# --description--

Acha $f(n)$ iwe nambari kubwa chanya $x$ chini ya ${10}^9$ ambapo nambari tisa za mwisho za $n^x$ zinaunda nambari $x$ (ikiwa ni pamoja na sifuri za mbele), au sifuri ikiwa hakuna nambari kama hiyo.

Kwa mfano:

$$\begin{align}
  & f(4) = 411\\,728\\,896 (4^{411\\,728\\,896} = ...490\underline{411728896}) \\\\
  & f(10) = 0 \\\\
  & f(157) = 743\\,757 (157^{743\\,757} = ...567\underline{000743757}) \\\\
  & Σf(n), 2 ≤ n ≤ 103 = 442\\,530\\,011\\,399
\end{align}$$

Tafuta $\sum f(n)$, $2 ≤ n ≤ {10}^6$.
