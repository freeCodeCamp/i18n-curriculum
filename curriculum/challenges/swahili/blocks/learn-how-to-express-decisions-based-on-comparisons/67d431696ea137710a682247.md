# --explanation--

`Value` linamaanisha kufikiri kitu ni muhimu. Kwa mfano: 

`She values teamwork because she believes working together is the best way to succeed.` - Hii ina maana mtu anadhani kazi ya pamoja ni muhimu sana.

`Creativity` ni uwezo wa kuja na mawazo mapya na ya kipekee. Kwa mfano:  

`His creativity helped him design a unique logo for the company.` - Hii ina maana mtu alitumia mawazo yake kuunda kitu kipya.

`Innovation` linamaanisha kuanzisha mawazo au maboresho mapya. Kwa mfano:  

`The company's innovation led to a new kind of smartphone with better battery life.` - Hii ina maana kampuni ilitengeneza simu janja ambayo ni mpya na bora zaidi kuliko awali.
