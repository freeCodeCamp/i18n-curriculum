# --explanation--

`To ensure` ina maana ya kuhakikisha jambo fulani linatokea au kufanikishwa. Kwa mfano:

`We must ensure that all safety regulations are followed.` - Hii ina maana ya kuhakikisha kanuni zinafuatwa.

`To be on track` ina maana ya kuendelea kama ilivyopangwa au kutarajiwa. Kwa mfano:

`The project is on track to be completed by the deadline.` - Hii ina maana mradi unaendelea kama ilivyotarajiwa na huenda ukamalizika kwa wakati.

Katika muktadha huu, Bob anataka kuthibitisha kuwa Jessica anafikia malengo yake.
