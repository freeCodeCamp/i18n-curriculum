# --description--

`To skim` inamaanisha kusoma au kuangalia kitu kwa haraka ili kupata mawazo makuu bila kuwa makini na maelezo ya kina. Kwa mfano, `She skimmed the article to understand the general topic.`

`To skim through` ni sawa, ikimaanisha kupitia haraka maandishi au hati. Kama vile, `He skimmed through the manual to find the troubleshooting section.`
