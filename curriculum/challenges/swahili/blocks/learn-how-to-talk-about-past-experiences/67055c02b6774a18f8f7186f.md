- `Please enter your credentials to log in to the server.` - maana ni kwamba unapaswa kuingiza taarifa zako ili kupata upatikanaji wa seva.

- `The developers released a patch to fix the bug.` - maana walirekebisha hitilafu kupitia sasisho la mfumo.

- `Make sure to apply the latest security patch to protect your system.` - maana sasisho la mwisho la usalama litakuwa na uwezo wa kulinda mfumo wako vizuri zaidi.

Hapa, James anaeleza kwamba tatizo lilitokana na kusahau kusasisha `credentials` za hifadhidata baada ya kutumia `patch` la usalama.
