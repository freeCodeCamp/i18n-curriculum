`She finished the report and emailed the rest of the documents.` – Hii inamaanisha alituma mafaili yaliyobaki ambayo hayakutumwa bado.

`I'll handle the client meeting this afternoon.` – Hii inamaanisha msemaji atashughulikia mkutano.
