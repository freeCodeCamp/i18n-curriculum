# --explanation--

A frase `I've noticed that` é usada para introduzir uma observação.  The `Present Perfect` tense is used here because, even though Bob's observation happened in the past, it is still relevant in the present, as this is something she could improve and it affects Jessica's current review. Por exemplo:

`I've noticed that the reports contain a few errors.` - Isso significa que o falante observou erros ao longo do tempo - o problema ainda existe.
