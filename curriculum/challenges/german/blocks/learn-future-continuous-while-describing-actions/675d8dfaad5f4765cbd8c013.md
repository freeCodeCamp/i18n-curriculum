## --text--

Welcher Satz verwendet `don't` korrekt in einer Tag-Frage?

## --answers--

`She likes coffee, don't she?`

### --feedback--

Das Subjekt `she` erfordert `doesn't`, nicht `don't`.

---

`We need to resolve this quickly, don't we?`

---

`They know the answer, don't it?`

### --feedback--

Das Subjekt `they` erfordert `don't they`, nicht `don't it`.

---

`The system is working fine, don't it?`

### --feedback--

Das Subjekt `it` erfordert `doesn't`, nicht `don't`.

2

# --explanation--

Tag-Fragen müssen zum Subjekt und Hilfsverb des Hauptsatzes passen.
