# --explanation--

`我 (wǒ)` inamaanisha "Mimi", `也 (yě)` inamaanisha "pia" au "basi", na `是 (shì)` inamaanisha "kuwa". Pamoja, zinaunda msemo `我也是 (wǒ yě shì)` unaodhihirisha kwa asili kufanana katika mazungumzo.

Kwa mfano, kama mtu anasema `我很开心认识你 (wǒ hěn kāi xīn rèn shì nǐ)` ikimaanisha "Nafurahi sana kukutana nawe", kujibu kwa `我也是 (wǒ yě shì)` kunaonyesha kwamba nawe unahisi furaha ya kukutana naye.
