# --explanation--

- `I know we added a lot of new features, but these need to be tested before release.` - Hapa, `these` inarejelea `features` zilizotajwa awali.

`Customer satisfaction` inahusu jinsi wateja wanavyofurahia au kuridhika na bidhaa au huduma. Kwa mfano:

`Improving response time in customer support helps increase customer satisfaction.` - Hii ina maana kwamba majibu ya haraka husababisha wateja kuwa na furaha zaidi.
