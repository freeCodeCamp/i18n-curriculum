# --description--

Kuelewa jinsi ya dhihirisha kukubaliana au kutokukubaliana ni muhimu katika mazungumzo yoyote.

Kusema `agree` kunamaanisha kuwa na maoni sawa na mtu mwingine. Kwa mfano, `I agree with your idea to increase marketing efforts.`

Kusema `disagree` kunamaanisha kuwa na maoni tofauti. Kwa mfano, `I disagree with the decision to postpone the project.`

Pia ni muhimu kujua jinsi ya kutokukubaliana `respectfully`. Hii inamaanisha unadhihirisha maoni yako tofauti kwa njia ya heshima na inayozingatia hisia za mtu mwingine. Kwa mfano, `I respectfully disagree with your approach, but I see your point.` au `I don't see it that way, but I understand your perspective.`
