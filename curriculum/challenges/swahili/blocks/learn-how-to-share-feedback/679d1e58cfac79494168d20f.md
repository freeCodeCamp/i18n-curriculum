# --explanation--

`To point out` inamaanisha kuangazia au kuonyesha jambo fulani. Katika kesi hii, mteja `pointed out` matatizo na kitendakazi cha utafutaji na vitufe. Mfano mwingine:

`She pointed out that the report had incorrect data.` - Hii inamaanisha alionyesha hitilafu katika ripoti.

`To respond` inamaanisha kutenda au kufanya kazi kama inavyotarajiwa. Ikiwa vitufe havijibu, inamaanisha havifanyi kazi wakati vinabofyiwa. Kwa mfano:

`My phone isn't responding when I tap the screen.` - Hii inamaanisha skrini haijibu mguso.
