## --text--

Huu ni ujumbe ambao Jake alitumia kwa wateja baada ya shambulio la ulaghai wa mtandao (phishing):

`We're currently addressing the issue and taking all necessary steps to secure the system.`  

`We've run a full system scan to identify hidden malware, and the IT support team is actively monitoring the situation.`  

`I'll provide you with regular updates to ensure transparency.`  

`Please let us know if you have further concerns.`  

Wateja walijibu:  

`We're very concerned about the security of our data. Can you confirm when this issue will be fully resolved?`

Nini wasiwasi mkuu wa wateja?

# --explanation--

Wateja wanasema, `We're very concerned about the security of our data.` 

Hii inaonyesha wazi kuwa wasiwasi wao mkuu ni **usalama wa data zao**. 

Wanauliza, `Can you confirm when this issue will be fully resolved?` jambo linaloashiria wanatafuta uhakikisho kuwa data zao ziko salama na tatizo litarekebishwa hivi karibuni. Kwa mfano:  

- `We're worried about losing sensitive information.` - Hii inaonyesha wasiwasi kuhusu usalama wa data.  

- `Is the system safe now?` - Hii inaonyesha hitaji la uthibitisho kuhusu azimio.
