# --description--

`He's` inaweza kuwa kifupisho cha `he has` au `he is`. Unaweza kuelewa ni ipi kwa kuangalia muktadha:

- Ikiwa `he's` inafuatiwa na kitenzi katika hali ya zamani iliyokamilika (kama `been`, `done` au `seen`), inamaanisha `he has`. Kwa mfano, `he's been working` inamaanisha `he has been working`.

- Ikiwa `he's` inafuatiwa na kivumishi au kitenzi katika hali ya sasa (kama `tired`, `going` au `sleeping`), inamaanisha `he is`. Kwa mfano, `he's tired` inamaanisha `he is tired`.

Kuelewa hili kunakusaidia kujua hasa mtu anamaanisha nini anaposema `he's`.
