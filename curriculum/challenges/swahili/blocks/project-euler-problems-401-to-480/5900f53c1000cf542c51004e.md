# --description--

Kitendakazi $f$ kimefafanuliwa kwa nambari kamili chanya zote kama ifuatavyo:

$$\begin{align}
  & f(1) = 1 \\\\
  & f(3) = 3 \\\\
  & f(2n) = f(n) \\\\
  & f(4n + 1) = 2f(2n + 1) - f(n) \\\\
  & f(4n + 3) = 3f(2n + 1) - 2f(n)
\end{align}$$

Kitendakazi $S(n)$ kimefafanuliwa kama $\sum_{i=1}^{n} f(i)$.

$S(8) = 22$ na $S(100) = 3604$.

Tafuta $S(3^{37})$. Toa tarakimu 9 za mwisho za jibu lako.
