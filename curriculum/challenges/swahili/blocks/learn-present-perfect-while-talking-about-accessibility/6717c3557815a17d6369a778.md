`Haven't` ni kifupisho cha `have not`, kinachotumika katika wakati wa `Present Perfect` kuonyesha kuwa kitendo hakijatokea.

`The user left a negative review about the app's performance.` - Hapa, `negative` linaelezea asili isiyopendeza ya uhakiki.

`I received a comment suggesting more features in the app.` - Katika muktadha huu, `comment` linaelezea mrejesho uliotolewa na mtumizi kuhusu programu.
