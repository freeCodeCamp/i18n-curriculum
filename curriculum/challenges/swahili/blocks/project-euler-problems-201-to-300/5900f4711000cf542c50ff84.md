# --description--

Tuitaje nambari kamili chanya $k$ kuwa mraba-msingi, ikiwa kuna jozi ya nambari kamili $m > 0$ na $n ≥ k$, ambapo jumla ya mraba ($m + 1$) mfululizo hadi $k$ ni sawa na jumla ya mraba $m$ mfululizo kuanzia ($n + 1$):

$${(k - m)}^2 + \ldots + k^2 = {(n + 1)}^2 + \ldots + {(n + m)}^2$$

Baadhi ya mraba-msingi wadogo ni

$$\begin{align}
  & \mathbf{4}: 3^2 + \mathbf{4}^2 = 5^2 \\\\
  & \mathbf{21}: {20}^2 + \mathbf{21}^2 = {29}^2 \\\\
  & \mathbf{24}: {21}^2 + {22}^2 + {23}^2 + \mathbf{24}^2 = {25}^2 + {26}^2 + {27}^2 \\\\
  & \mathbf{110}: {108}^2 + {109}^2 + \mathbf{110}^2 = {133}^2 + {134}^2 \\\\
\end{align}$$

Tafuta jumla ya mraba-msingi wote tofauti $≤ {10}^{10}$.
