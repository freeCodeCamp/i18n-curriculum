# --description--

When you speak about what another person does every day, like your friend or a teacher, and you use `he`, `she`, or `it`, you will often need to conjugate the verb of your sentence.

Como ya estudiaste, la conjugación para la mayoría de los verbos puede ser simple. You just add `-s` or `-es` to the end of the verb. For example:

Verb `to talk` conjugation:

- `I talk`, `you talk`, `we talk` y `they talk`.

- `He talks`, `she talks` y `it talks`.

Verb `to do` conjugation:

- `I do`, `you do`, `we do` y `they do`.

- `He does`, `she does` y `it does`.
