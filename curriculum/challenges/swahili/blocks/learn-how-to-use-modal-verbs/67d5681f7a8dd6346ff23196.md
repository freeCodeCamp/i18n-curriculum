# --explanation--

Kumbuka:

`Must` hudhihirisha hitaji kali au wajibu. Kwa mfano:

`You must wear a seatbelt.`

`Have to` pia hudhihirisha wajibu lakini wakati mwingine si mkali kama must. Kwa mfano:

`You have to submit the report by Friday.`

`Should` hutumika kwa mapendekezo au ushauri. Kwa mfano:

`You should review your notes before the test.`
