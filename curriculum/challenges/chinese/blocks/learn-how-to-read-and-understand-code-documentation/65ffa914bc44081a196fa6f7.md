# --description--

`To skim` 意味着快速读取或浏览某物，以获取主要思想而不关注细节。例如，`She skimmed the article to understand the general topic.`

`To skim through` 类似，意思是快速浏览文本或文档。比如，`He skimmed through the manual to find the troubleshooting section.`
