# --description--

`Strongly` hutumika kuonyesha kiwango kikubwa cha uhakika au imani katika jambo fulani. Kwa mfano, `I strongly believe that this plan will work` inaonyesha kujiamini sana katika mpango.

`Strengths` inahusu vipengele vizuri au vyenye nguvu vya kitu au mtu. Kama kusema, `One of our team's strengths is our creativity.`

# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia `strongly` na `strengths` kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`I weakly believe in our team's weaknesses.`

### --feedback--

`Weakly` ni kinyume cha `strongly`, na `weaknesses` ni kinyume cha `strengths`.
