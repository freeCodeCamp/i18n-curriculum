# --description--

The simple past tense in English is used for actions or situations that were completed in the past.Verbros regulares no passado geralmente terminam com `-ed`, enquanto verbos irregulares têm formas passadas únicas.Por exemplo, como você aprendeu em tarefas anteriores, `was` e `were` são as formas no passado de `is` e `are`.

Exemplos de passado simples:

- `I worked at a company.` (`work` se torna `worked`)
- `I was happy.` (`is` becomes `was`)
- `Estávamos ocupados.` (`are` becomes `were`)
