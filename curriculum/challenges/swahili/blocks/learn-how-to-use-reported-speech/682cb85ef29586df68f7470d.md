# --explanation--

`Damage` inahusu madhara au athari mbaya zilizotokea kwa kitu. Kwa mfano:

`The storm caused serious damage to the roof.` – Hii ina maana kwamba dhoruba iliharibu paa.

`To mention` ni kitenzi kingine kinachotumika katika **usemi ulioripotiwa**, kama `say` au `tell`. Kina maana ya kurejelea kitu katika mazungumzo.

Katika usemi ulioripotiwa, viwakilishi mara nyingi hubadilika. Kwa mfano, katika usemi wa moja kwa moja, Jake angeweza kusema: `We managed to isolate the affected systems within minutes`. Hata hivyo, wakati Sophie anaripoti, `we` hubadilika kuwa `they` kwa sababu hakuwa sehemu ya jitihada: `Jake mentioned that they managed to isolate the affected systems`. Mfano mwingine:

- Usemi wa moja kwa moja: `We completed the project on time.`

- Usemi ulioripotiwa: `She mentioned that they had completed the project on time.`
