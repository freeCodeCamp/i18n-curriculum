# --explanation--

Ili kuelewa maoni ya Jake, sikiliza usemi `firewall rules can sometimes be misconfigured.`

`Misconfigured` inamaanisha kuanzishwa kwa njia isiyo sahihi. Kwa mfano:

`The server went down because of a misconfigured setting.` – Hii ina maana mtu alikosea wakati wa kuanzisha mfumo.

`Sometimes` inaonyesha kwamba hili halitokei kila wakati, lakini linawezekana. Jake anasema kwamba sheria za firewall si kamilifu — makosa yanaweza kutokea.
