# --explanation--

Muundo `to be` + kivumishi + `to` + kitenzi katika umbo la infinitive hutumika kuelezea hisia, hali, au msimamo wa mtu kuhusu tendo fulani. Kwa mfano:

- `She was surprised to receive such a thoughtful gift.` - Hapa, `surprised` ni kivumishi, na `to receive` ni kitenzi katika umbo la infinitive.

- `He was excited to join the new project.` - Katika sentensi hii, `excited` anaonyesha hamu yake, na `to join` ni tendo analotamani kufanya.

Kwa kusema alichosema, Jessica anadhihirisha hisia zake kuhusiana na kile Bob alichosema kuhusu kazi yake ya QA.
