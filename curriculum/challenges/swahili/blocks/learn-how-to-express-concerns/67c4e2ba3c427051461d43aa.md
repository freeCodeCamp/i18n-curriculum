# --explanation--

`That's a good idea` ni njia ya kawaida ya kujibu swali la `Have you thought about...`. Inathibitisha pendekezo na inaonyesha kwamba msemaji analitambua kama wazo lenye msaada. Kwa mfano:

- **Tom:** `Have you thought about using a different strategy?` - Mtu huyu anapendekeza njia tofauti ya kutatua tatizo.

- **Rachel:** `That's a good idea! We should try it.` - Mtu huyu anakubali pendekezo na anataka kufanya kile alichosemwa na mtu mwingine.
