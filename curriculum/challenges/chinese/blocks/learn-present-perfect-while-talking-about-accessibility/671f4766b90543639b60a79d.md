## --text--

哪个选项使用了 `Present Perfect Continuous` 时态的否定形式？

## --answers--

`She hasn't been studying for the exam lately.`

---

`She hasn't studied for the exam yet.`

### --feedback--

这是 `Present Perfect` 时态的否定形式，而不是 `Present Perfect Continuous` 时态。

---

### --feedback--

### --feedback--

# --explanation--

`Present Perfect Continuous` 时态的否定形式由 `hasn't/haven't + been + present participle` 构成，表示一个持续的动作在一段时间内未按预期发生。例如：

`He hasn't been exercising regularly.` - 这意味着他的锻炼例程不规律。
  
`Present Perfect` 时态的否定形式使用 `hasn't/haven't + past participle`，描述一个直到现在为止尚未完成的动作。例如：

`He hasn't exercised this week.` - 这表示本周没有进行锻炼。
