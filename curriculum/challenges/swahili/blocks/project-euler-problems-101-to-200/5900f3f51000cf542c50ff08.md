# --description--

Tafakari mfululizo usio na kikomo wa polinomu $A_{F}(x) = xF_1 + x^2F_2 + x^3F_3 + \ldots$, ambapo $F_k$ ni kipengele cha $k$ katika mfululizo wa Fibonacci: $1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, \ldots$; yaani, $F_k = F_{k − 1} + F_{k − 2}, F_1 = 1$ na $F_2 = 1$.

Kwa changamoto hii tutakuwa na nia ya thamani za $x$ ambazo $A_{F}(x)$ ni nambari kamili chanya.

Kwa mshangao

$$\begin{align}
A_F(\frac{1}{2}) & = (\frac{1}{2}) × 1 + {(\frac{1}{2})}^2 × 1 + {(\frac{1}{2})}^3 × 2 + {(\frac{1}{2})}^4 × 3 + {(\frac{1}{2})}^5 × 5 + \cdots \\\\
                 & = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{4} + \frac{2}{8} + \frac{3}{16} + \frac{5}{32} + \cdots \\\\
                 & = 2
\end{align}$$

Thamani zinazolingana za $x$ kwa nambari tano za asili za kwanza zinaonyeshwa hapa chini.

| $x$                       | $A_F(x)$ |
|---------------------------|----------|
| $\sqrt{2} − 1$            | $1$      |
| $\frac{1}{2}$             | $2$      |
| $\frac{\sqrt{13} − 2}{3}$ | $3$      |
| $\frac{\sqrt{89} − 5}{8}$ | $4$      |
| $\frac{\sqrt{34} − 3}{5}$ | $5$      |

Tutaita $A_F(x)$ kipande cha dhahabu ikiwa $x$ ni nambari ya mwelekeo wa kawaida, kwa sababu vinazidi kuwa nadra; kwa mfano, kipande cha dhahabu cha 10 ni 74049690.

Tafuta kipande cha dhahabu cha 15.
