`Customer service is one area we need to improve.` – Hii ina maana sehemu ya huduma kwa wateja katika biashara inahitaji makini zaidi.

`The app didn't meet user expectations because of bugs.` – Hii ina maana watumizi walitarajia uzoefu bora zaidi kuliko walivyoupata.
