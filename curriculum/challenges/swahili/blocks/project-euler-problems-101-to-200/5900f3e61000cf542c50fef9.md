# --description--

Njia ya kawaida kabisa ya kuhesabu $n^{15}$ inahitaji mara kumi na nne za kuzidisha:

$$n × n × \ldots × n = n^{15}$$

Lakini kwa kutumia njia ya "binary" unaweza kuikokotoa kwa mara sita za kuzidisha:

$$\begin{align}
  & n × n = n^2\\\\
  & n^2 × n^2 = n^4\\\\
  & n^4 × n^4 = n^8\\\\
  & n^8 × n^4 = n^{12}\\\\
  & n^{12} × n^2 = n^{14}\\\\
  & n^{14} × n = n^{15}
\end{align}$$

Hata hivyo bado inawezekana kuikokotoa kwa mara tano tu za kuzidisha:

$$\begin{align}
  & n × n = n^2\\\\
  & n^2 × n = n^3\\\\
  & n^3 × n^3 = n^6\\\\
  & n^6 × n^6 = n^{12}\\\\
  & n^{12} × n^3 = n^{15}
\end{align}$$

Tutaeleza $m(k)$ kuwa kiwango cha chini cha mara za kuzidisha kinachohitajika kuhesabu $n^k$; kwa mfano $m(15) = 5$.

Kwa $1 ≤ k ≤ 200$, pata $\sum{m(k)}$.
