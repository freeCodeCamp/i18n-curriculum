# --explanation--

`The full scope of an issue` inahusu kuelewa kila kipengele cha tatizo, ikiwa ni pamoja na sababu na athari zake, kabla ya kuchukua hatua. Kwa mfano:

`Before fixing the bug, we need to assess the full scope of its impact on other features.` - Hii inamaanisha kuchambua jinsi hitilafu inavyoathiri sehemu tofauti za mfumo.

`Let's make sure` hutumika kusisitiza kuthibitisha au kukagua jambo kabla ya kuendelea. Kwa mfano:

`Let's make sure we have all the requirements before starting development.` - Hii inamaanisha wanapaswa kuthibitisha kuwa kila kitu kiko tayari kabla ya kuanza mchakato wa maendeleo.
