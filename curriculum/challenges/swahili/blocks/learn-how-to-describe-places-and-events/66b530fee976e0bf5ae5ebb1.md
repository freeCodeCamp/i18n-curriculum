`Extra` linamaanisha ziada au zaidi ya kawaida au inavyohitajika. Kwa mfano:

- `We need extra chairs for the meeting.` - Tunahitaji viti zaidi kuliko kawaida.

`Because` hutumika kutoa sababu ya jambo fulani. Kwa mfano:

- `We canceled the meeting because of bad weather.` - Hali mbaya ya hewa ndiyo sababu ya kufuta mkutano.

- `The recent news was surprising.` - Habari zilikuja hivi karibuni.

- `The virus is a threat to our system.` - Hii inamaanisha virusi linaweza kusababisha madhara kwa mfumo.
