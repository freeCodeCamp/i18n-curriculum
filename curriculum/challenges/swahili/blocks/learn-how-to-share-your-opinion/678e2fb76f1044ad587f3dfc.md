`The developers spent the weekend refactoring the code to make it more readable and efficient.` - Hii ina maana kwamba wasanidi programu waliboresha muundo wa msimbo bila kubadilisha tabia yake.

`The codebase of the new project is large, and it will take time to review all the files.` - Hii ina maana kwamba mkusanyiko wa msimbo wote wa mradi ni mkubwa na utahitaji muda mwingi kupitia.
