## --text--

哪一句正確使用 `during` 來表示時間？

## --answers--

`He arrived during the door.`

### --feedback--

`During` 應該指涉一段時間，而不是一個物件。

---

`She walked during the park.`

### --feedback--

`During` 不應該用於這樣的地點。

---

`We had several meetings during the afternoon.`

---

`They spoke during each other loudly.`

### --feedback--

聽起來意思應該是 `to` 或 `with`。

## --video-solution--

3

# --explanation--

`During` 用來表示某事發生在特定時間期間內。它後面會接時間詞，例如 `the afternoon`、`the meeting` 或 `the week`。

在句子 `We had several meetings during the afternoon` 中，表示會議發生在下午的某個時間影格內。
