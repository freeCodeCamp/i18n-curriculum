# --description--

`Anyone who` 用于参考满足特定条件或要求的任何人。 

在技术领域，它通常用来描述具有某些技能或知识的人。例如，`Anyone who understands JavaScript can modify this script.`

# --questions--

## --text--

哪句话在技术上下文中正确使用了 `Anyone who`？

## --answers--

`Anyone who has access to the server can upload the files.`

---

`Anyone who please fix this bug.`

### --feedback--

语法正确的用法是，`Anyone who knows the solution, please fix this bug.`

---

`Anyone who the programmer is busy now.`

### --feedback--

这个句子语法不正确。一个更好的句子是，`Anyone who is a programmer is busy now.`

---

`Who anyone can join the meeting is not clear.`

### --feedback--

这句话结构不正确。正确的形式是，`It is not clear who can join the meeting.`
