# --description--

Hadi sasa, umejifunza jinsi ya kuuliza maswali kwa kutumia `do` / `does` na kutengeneza sentensi hasi kwa kutumia `don't` / `doesn't`. Hebu tukague tena. Kumbuka:

- Thibitisho: `He does work on weekends.` (inamaanisha mtu anafanya kazi wikendi)

- Hasi: `He doesn't work on weekends.` (inamaanisha mtu hafanyi kazi wikendi)

- Swali: `Does he work on weekends?` (inauliza kama mtu anafanya kazi wikendi)

# --instructions--

Soma sehemu ya mazungumzo na jaza mapengo kwa kutumia `do` au `don't`.

# --fillInTheBlank--

## --sentence--

`Tom: How about you? BLANK you have any hobbies?`

`Sophie: That's cool. I like photography, but I BLANK think of it as a hobby.`

## --blanks--

`Do`

### --feedback--

Tom anauliza swali kuhusu burudani za Sophie, ambalo linahitaji `do`.

---

`don't`

### --feedback--

Sophie anatoa tamko hasi kuhusu kutochukulia jambo fulani kama burudani, ambalo linahitaji `don't`.
