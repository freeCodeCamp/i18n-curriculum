# --explanation--

`Need to be careful` inamaanisha kuzingatia ili kuepuka makosa au matatizo. Ni tofauti na `need to care`. Wakati `need to care` inamaanisha kuwa wasiwasi au kuzingatia ni muhimu, `need to be careful` inamaanisha kuwa mtu anapaswa kuwa makini. Msisitizo uko zaidi kwa mtu kuliko kitendo cha kujali yenyewe. Kwa mfano:  

- `We need to have patience while waiting for results.` - Hapa, kitendo cha kuwa na subira kinahesabiwa.  

- `We need to be patient while waiting for results.` - Hapa, msisitizo uko kwa watu ambao wanapaswa kuwa na subira - `we`, katika kesi hii.
