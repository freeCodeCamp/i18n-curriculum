# --description--

Maneno `before` na `after` yanatumika kuelezea mfululizo wa matukio kwa wakati. 

`Before` inatumika kuzungumzia jambo linalotokea mapema zaidi kuliko tukio jingine, na `after` inatumika kwa jambo linalotokea baadaye. Kwa mfano:

- `I brush my teeth before I have breakfast.` - Kupiga mswaki hutokea kwanza.

- `I go to work after I have breakfast.` - Kwenda kazini hutokea baadaye.
