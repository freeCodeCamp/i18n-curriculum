# --description--

Acha $H$ iwe hyperbola iliyoainishwa na usemi wa hesabu $12x^2 + 7xy - 12y^2 = 625$.

Kisha, fafanua $X$ kama pointi (7, 1). Inaonekana kwamba $X$ iko katika $H$.

Sasa tutaainisha mfululizo wa pointi katika $H, \\{P_i : i ≥ 1\\}$, kama:

- $P_1 = (13, \frac{61}{4})$.
- $P_2 = (\frac{-43}{6}, -4)$.
- Kwa $i > 2$, $P_i$ ni pointi ya kipekee katika $H$ ambayo ni tofauti na $P_{i - 1}$ na kwamba mstari $P_iP_{i - 1}$ uko sambamba na mstari $P_{i - 2}X$. Inaweza kuonyeshwa kwamba $P_i$ imefafanuliwa vizuri, na kwamba kuratibu zake daima ni za kihesabu.

<img alt="animation showing defining points P_1 to P_6" src="https://cdn.freecodecamp.org/curriculum/project-euler/sequence-of-points-on-a-hyperbola.gif" style="background-color: white; padding: 10px; display: block; margin-right: auto; margin-left: auto; margin-bottom: 1.2rem;">

Umepatiwa kwamba $P_3 = (\frac{-19}{2}, \frac{-229}{24})$, $P_4 = (\frac{1267}{144}, \frac{-37}{12})$ na $P_7 = (\frac{17\\,194\\,218\\,091}{143\\,327\\,232}, \frac{274\\,748\\,766\\,781}{1\\,719\\,926\\,784})$.

Tafuta $P_n$ kwa $n = {11}^{14}$ kwa muundo ufuatao: Ikiwa $P_n = (\frac{a}{b}, \frac{c}{d})$ ambapo sehemu ni katika hali ya chini kabisa na vizingiti ni chanya, basi jibu ni $(a + b + c + d)\bmod 1\\,000\\,000\\,007$.

Kwa $n = 7$, jibu lingekuwa: $806\\,236\\,837$.
