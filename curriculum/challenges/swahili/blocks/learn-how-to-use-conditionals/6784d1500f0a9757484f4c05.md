# --explanation--

Neno `shock` linaelezea hisia kali za mshangao au huzuni kutokana na jambo lisilotarajiwa. Kwa mfano:

- `The user feedback came as a shock; they found the app too complicated.` - Timu haikuamini watumizi wangepata ugumu kutumia programu.

- `The company's decision to shut down the service was a shock to its users.` - Watumizi walishangazwa na kukata tamaa wakati huduma ilipotolewa.

Katika sauti, Tom analitumia kuonyesha jinsi ufungaji wa TechCorp ungekuwa jambo lisilotarajiwa na lenye athari kubwa.
