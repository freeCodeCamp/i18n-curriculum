`We might have to update the software to fix the issue.` - Timu inaweza kuhitaji kusahihisha programu ili kutatua tatizo.

`I had to escalate the problem because it wasn't resolved at the lower level.` - Ulihitaji kutuma suala kwa mtu wa ngazi ya juu kwa sababu halikutatuliwa na timu.
