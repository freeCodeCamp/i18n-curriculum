# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia `left side` au `right side` kwa usahihi kuelezea mahali?

## --answers--

`The meeting room is of the right side of the hallway.`

### --feedback--

Inapaswa kuwa `on the right side`, si `of the right side`.

---

`The kitchen is right side on the building.`

### --feedback--

Fomu sahihi ni `on the right side of the building`.

---

`The server room is on the right side of the hallway.`

---

`The printer is at the left side hallway.`

### --feedback--

Inapaswa kuwa `on the left side of the hallway`.

## --video-solution--

3

# --explanation--

`On the [left/right] side of [place]` ndiyo njia sahihi na ya asili ya kuelezea aina hii ya mwelekeo.

Sentensi sahihi inatumia `on the right side of the hallway`, ambayo ni muundo unaofaa kuelezea mahali pa kimwili.

Makosa ya kawaida ni pamoja na kutumia `of the right side`, au kuchanganya mpangilio wa maneno na vitenzi vya nafasi.
