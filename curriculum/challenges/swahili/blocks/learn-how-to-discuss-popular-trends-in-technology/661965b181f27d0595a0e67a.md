# --description--

`To be familiar` na kitu ni kujua angalau kidogo kuhusu mada hiyo. Ikiwa unauliza mtu `are you familiar with this?`, unawauliza kama wanajua kuhusu hilo. Mfano: `I have never seen this soda before. It's delicious. Are you familiar with it?` (unauliza kama mtu anajua na/au amewahi kuonja soda hiyo hapo awali).
