# --description--

Usemi `you're doing great` ni njia ya kumwambia mtu anafanya vizuri sana au anasimamia hali fulani kwa mafanikio. Ni aina ya kuhimiza au kusifu. Kwa mfano, `After seeing how well you organized the event, I must say, you're doing great!`

Baada yake, unaweza kutumia misemo inayotangulia na kitenzi, inayomalizika kwa `-ing`, inayobainisha kile mtu anachofanya vizuri. Kwa mfano, `You're doing great keeping up with your studies.`

`Deal with` ina maana ya kusimamia au kushughulikia jambo fulani, hasa tatizo au changamoto. Inahusisha kuchukua hatua za kushughulikia hali au tatizo. Kwa mfano, `She has to deal with a lot of emails every morning.`

Usemi `at the same time` unaonyesha kwamba matendo au matukio mawili au zaidi yanatokea pamoja, siyo kando kando.
