# --description--

Acha $R(M, N)$ iwe idadi ya nukta za kimiani ($x$, $y$) zinazokidhi $M \lt x \le N$, $M \lt y \le N$ na $\left\lfloor\frac{y^2}{x^2}\right\rfloor$ ni nambari isiyo sawa.

Tunaweza kuthibitisha kuwa $R(0, 100) = 3\\,019$ na $R(100, 10\\,000) = 29\\,750\\,422$.

Tafuta $R(2 \times {10}^6, {10}^9)$.

**Kumbuka:** $\lfloor x\rfloor$ inawakilisha kitendakazi cha sakafu (floor function).
