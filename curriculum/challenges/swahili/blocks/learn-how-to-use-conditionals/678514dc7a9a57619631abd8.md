# --explanation--

Katika maswali ya lebo kama `wouldn't they?`, mzungumzaji kawaida anatafuta uthibitisho au makubaliano. Jibu kama `That's right` hutoa uthibitisho wazi na huweka mazungumzo yakiwa sawa.

Mifano mingine ya majibu ya kuthibitisha:

- `Yes, exactly.`

- `Absolutely, you're correct.`

- `I agree completely.`

Majibu haya yanatambua na kuthibitisha kauli ya mzungumzaji.
