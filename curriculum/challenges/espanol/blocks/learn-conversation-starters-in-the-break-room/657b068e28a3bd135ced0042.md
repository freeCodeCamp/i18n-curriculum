# --description--

`So far` means up to this moment or until now. Se utiliza cuando se habla de un tiempo que comenzó en el pasado y continúa hasta el presente. If someone asks, `Are you happy with the workplace so far?`, they want to know if you liked your workplace from the first day up to today.

Another example: if you say, `The project is good so far`, it means from the beginning of the project until now, everything is good.

In the dialogue, Sarah asks Tom if he is happy with his new job so far. Ella está preguntando sobre su tiempo en el trabajo desde el principio hasta su conversación.
