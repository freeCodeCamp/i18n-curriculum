## --text--

你正在向你的经理汇报新建办公室的版型。哪句话正确描述了某个部门或房间的位置？

## --answers--

`The break room is between the IT department and the kitchen.`

---

`The meeting room is between of the lobby and the copy area.`

### --feedback--

`Between of` 不是正确的短语。它应该是 `between the lobby and the copy area`。

---

`Reception is between the sales team with the HR office.`

### --feedback--

你应该使用 `between ... and ...`，而不是 `between ... with ...`

---

`The server room is in between to the hallway and the printer area.`

### --feedback--

它应该在 `between the hallway and the printer area` 之间，不带 `to`。

1

# --explanation--

`Between ... and ...` 用于描述位于两个特定地点中间的位置。

正确的句子遵循此结构：`between the IT department and the kitchen`。

其他选项误用介词或连接词，导致语法错误。
