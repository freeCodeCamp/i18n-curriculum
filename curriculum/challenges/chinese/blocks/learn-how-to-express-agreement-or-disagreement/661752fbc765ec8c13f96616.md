# --description--

理解如何表达同意或不同意在任何对话中都至关重要。 

`agree` 意味着与你的观点相同。例如，`I agree with your idea to increase marketing efforts.` 

`disagree` 意味着有不同的意见。例如，`I disagree with the decision to postpone the project.`

了解如何`respectfully`地表达不同意见也很重要。这意味着你以礼貌且考虑对方感受的方式表达你的不同观点。例如，`I respectfully disagree with your approach, but I see your point.` 或者 `I don't see it that way, but I understand your perspective.`

# --questions--

## --text--

哪句话显示了尊重的反对方式？

## --answers--

`You're wrong, and I can't believe you think that way.`

### --feedback--

这个响应是直接且对抗性的，不是尊重的异议方式。

---

`I don't see it that way, but I understand your perspective.`

---

`That's a terrible idea, and I won't support it.`

### --feedback--

这条语句直白，可能被视为不尊重。这不是表达不同意见的礼貌方式。

---

`I completely disagree and you should reconsider your position.`

### --feedback--

虽然这表达了不同意见，但语气可能被认为是命令式而非尊重。
