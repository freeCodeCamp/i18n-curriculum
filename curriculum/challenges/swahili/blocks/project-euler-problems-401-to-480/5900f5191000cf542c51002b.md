# --description--

Acha $a$, $b$ na $c$ ziwe nambari chanya.

Acha $W$, $X$, $Y$, $Z$ ziwe nukta nne za mstari mmoja ambapo $|WX| = a$, $|XY| = b$, $|YZ| = c$ na $|WZ| = a + b + c$.

Acha $C_{\text{in}}$ iwe duara lenye kipenyo $XY$.

Acha $C_{\text{out}}$ iwe duara lenye kipenyo $WZ$.

Trojia ($a$, $b$, $c$) huitwa *trojia ya mnyororo* ikiwa unaweza kuweka miduara $k ≥ 3$ tofauti $C_1, C_2, \ldots, C_k$ kwa namna ifuatayo:

- $C_i$ haina nukta za ndani zinazoshirikiana na $C_j$ yoyote kwa $1 ≤ i$, $j ≤ k$ na $i ≠ j$,
- $C_i$ inagusa $C_{\text{in}}$ na $C_{\text{out}}$ kwa $1 ≤ i ≤ k$,
- $C_i$ inagusa $C_{i + 1}$ kwa $1 ≤ i < k$, na
- $C_k$ inagusa $C_1$.

Kwa mfano, (5, 5, 5) na (4, 3, 21) ni trojia za mnyororo, wakati inaweza kuonyeshwa kuwa (2, 2, 5) siyo.

<img alt="wakilisho wa trojia ya mnyororo" src="https://cdn.freecodecamp.org/curriculum/project-euler/necklace-of-circles.png" style="background-color: white; padding: 10px; display: block; margin-right: auto; margin-left: auto; margin-bottom: 1.2rem;">

Acha $T(n)$ iwe idadi ya trojia za mnyororo $(a, b, c)$ ambapo $a$, $b$ na $c$ ni nambari kamili chanya, na $b ≤ n$. Kwa mfano, $T(1) = 9$, $T(20) = 732$ na $T(3\\,000) = 438\\,106$.

Tafuta $T(1\\,000\\,000\\,000)$.
