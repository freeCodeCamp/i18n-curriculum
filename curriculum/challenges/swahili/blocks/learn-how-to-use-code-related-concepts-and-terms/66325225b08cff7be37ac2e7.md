# --description--

Viwakilishi vinavyohusiana kama `who`, `whom`, `which`, na `that` hutumika kuunganisha vifungu vinavyohusiana na nomino au kivumishi.

`Who` na `whom` vinarejelea watu. Tofauti ni kwamba `who` ni mhusika wa kitenzi, wakati `whom` hutumika kama kipengele cha kitenzi au kiambatisho cha kitenzi.

`Which` hutumika kwa vitu maalum au wanyama, na `that` inaweza kutumika kwa watu, vitu, au wanyama katika vifungu vinavyoelezea.

Mifano:

`The programmer who wrote this code is brilliant.` (`who` inarejelea mpangaji programu)

`The artist whom we met at the gallery is internationally recognized.` (`whom` inarejelea msanii)

`The tool that you lent me was useful.` (`that` inarejelea chombo)

`The book, which I bought yesterday, is already a bestseller.` (`which` inarejelea kitabu)
