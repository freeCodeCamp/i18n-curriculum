# --description--

`Indoor` inahusu shughuli zinazofanywa ndani ya jengo, wakati `outdoor` inamaanisha shughuli zinazofanywa nje. Kwa mfano, `Indoor activities include reading and cooking. Outdoor activities involve hiking and gardening`. Hapa, `indoor` inamaanisha ndani, na `outdoor` inamaanisha nje.

`Though` ni usemi wa kihesabu unaotumika kuonyesha tofauti kati ya sentensi mbili, sawa na `but`. Pia unaweza kutumika katikati au mwishoni mwa sentensi, si tu mwanzoni. Kwa mfano, `I know we wanted to travel. We don't have the money for the tickets, though`. Hapa, `though` inaonyesha tofauti kati ya matakwa na halisi.

`I suppose...` hutumika kuonyesha dhana ya upungufu au makubaliano ya kiasi. Kwa mfano, `I suppose we could meet tomorrow`. Hii inaonyesha makubaliano, lakini si makubwa sana.
