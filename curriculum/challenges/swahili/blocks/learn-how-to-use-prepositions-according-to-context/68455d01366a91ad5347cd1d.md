# --questions--

## --text--

Unamripoti meneja wako kuhusu mpangilio mpya wa ofisi. Ni sentensi gani inayoelezea kwa usahihi mahali pa idara au chumba?

## --answers--

`The break room is between the IT department and the kitchen.`

---

`The meeting room is between of the lobby and the copy area.`

### --feedback--

`Between of` si msemo sahihi. Inapaswa kuwa `between the lobby and the copy area` tu.

---

`Reception is between the sales team with the HR office.`

### --feedback--

Unapaswa kutumia `between ... and ...`, si `between ... with ...`

---

`The server room is in between to the hallway and the printer area.`

### --feedback--

Inapaswa kuwa `between the hallway and the printer area` bila `to`.

1

# --explanation--

`Between ... and ...` hutumika kuelezea mahali palipo katikati ya maeneo mawili maalum.

Sentensi sahihi inafuata muundo huu kwa usahihi: `between the IT department and the kitchen`.

Chaguzi nyingine hutumia vibaya viunganishi au viambatanishi, na hivyo kuzifanya zisizo za kisarufi.
