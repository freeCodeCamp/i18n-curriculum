# --description--

Kuelewa tofauti kati ya `can` na `could` ni muhimu sana.

`Can` hutumika kuzungumzia kile mtu anaweza kufanya, kile kinachowezekana, au kuomba ruhusa kwa sasa au baadaye. Kwa mfano:

- `I can code very well.` - Hapa, `can` inaonyesha uwezo wa sasa.

- `Can I leave early today?` - Hapa, `can` inaomba ruhusa.

`Could` hutumika kwa njia tofauti. Inaweza kuonyesha uwezo wa zamani, kufanya ombi la heshima, au kupendekeza jambo kwa njia laini. Kwa mfano:

- `Could you pass me that tool, please?` — Hapa, `could` hufanya ombi la heshima.

- `When I first learned programming, I could spend hours debugging without getting tired.` — Hapa, `could` inaonyesha uwezo wa zamani.
