# --description--

Kwa nambari kamili zisizo hasi $m$, $n$, kitendakazi cha Ackermann $A(m, n)$ kimefafanuliwa kama ifuatavyo:

$$A(m, n) =
\begin{cases}
n + 1                 & \text{ikiwa $m = 0$}             \\\\
A(m - 1, 1)           & \text{ikiwa $m > 0$ na $n = 0$} \\\\
A(m - 1, A(m, n - 1)) & \text{ikiwa $m > 0$ na $n > 0$}
\end{cases}$$

Kwa mfano $A(1, 0) = 2$, $A(2, 2) = 7$ na $A(3, 4) = 125$.

Tafuta $\displaystyle\sum_{n = 0}^6 A(n, n)$ na toa jibu lako kwa mod ${14}^8$.
