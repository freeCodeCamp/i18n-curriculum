# --description--

`Let's get started` ni usemi unaotumika kuanzisha shughuli, mkutano, au kikao. Unaashiria kuwa ni wakati wa kuanza shughuli zilizopangwa.

Kwa mfano, mwanzoni mwa mkutano mtu anaweza kusema, `Let's get started with the first topic`.

# --questions--

## --text--

Usemi `Let's get started` kwa kawaida unamaanisha nini katika mkutano?

## --answers--

Ni ombi kwa kila mtu kuondoka kwenye mkutano.

### --feedback--

`Let's get started` inahusu kuanzisha kitu, si kumaliza au kuondoka.

---

### --feedback--

`Let's get started` hutumika kuanzisha kazi au shughuli, si kupumzika.

### --feedback--
