# --description--

了解如何表達同意或不同意在任何對話中都是關鍵。 

`agree` 意味著與他人有相同的意見。例如，`I agree with your idea to increase marketing efforts.` 

`disagree` 意味著有不同的意見。例如，`I disagree with the decision to postpone the project.`

了解如何 `respectfully` 表達不同意見也很重要。這表示你以禮貌且顧及對方感受的方式表達你的不同看法。 例如，`I respectfully disagree with your approach, but I see your point.` 或 `I don't see it that way, but I understand your perspective.`

# --questions--

## --text--

哪一句話顯示出尊重的不同意見方式？

## --answers--

`You're wrong, and I can't believe you think that way.`

### --feedback--

這種回應直接且對抗，並非尊重的反對方式。

---

`I don't see it that way, but I understand your perspective.`

---

`That's a terrible idea, and I won't support it.`

### --feedback--

這個述語直白，可能被視為不尊重。這不是表達不同意見的禮貌方式。

---

`I completely disagree and you should reconsider your position.`

### --feedback--

雖然這表達了不同意見，但語氣可能被視為命令式而非尊重。
