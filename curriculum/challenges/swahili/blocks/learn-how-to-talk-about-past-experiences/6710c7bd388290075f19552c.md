# --explanation--

`What's on your mind?` hutumika kumuuliza mtu anafikiria nini au kama ana wasiwasi au mawazo yoyote anayotaka kushiriki.

`You seem quiet today. What's on your mind?` - Unamuuliza mtu mwingine anafikiria nini.
