# --explanation--

`Not as much` ni usemi wa kihesabu wa kulinganisha unaotumika kuonyesha kwamba jambo fulani hutokea au kuwepo kwa kiwango kidogo zaidi kuliko lingine. Kwa mfano:

- `She studies a lot, but not as much as her brother.` – Hii ina maana anasoma kidogo kuliko kaka yake, ingawa anasoma sana.

- `The first product sold well, but not as much as the second one.` – Hii ina maana bidhaa ya pili iliuza zaidi kuliko ya kwanza, ingawa bidhaa ya kwanza iliuza vizuri.
