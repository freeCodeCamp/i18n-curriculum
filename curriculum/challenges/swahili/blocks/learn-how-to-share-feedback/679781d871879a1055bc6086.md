# --explanation--

Muundo `if` + `Present Simple` wakati, `will` + kitenzi cha msingi hutumika kuelezea sentensi ya masharti ambapo kitendo fulani husababisha matokeo yanayowezekana. Kwa mfano:

`If you practice every day, you will get better at coding.` - Kufanya mazoezi kila siku husababisha maendeleo.

`To follow someone's work` ina maana ya kuelewa au kufuatilia maendeleo, vitendo, au matokeo ya mtu. Kwa mfano:

`The new intern is following your work closely to learn from you.` - Hii ina maana kuwa mwanafunzi wa mafunzo anatazama na kuelewa unachofanya ili kupata maarifa.
