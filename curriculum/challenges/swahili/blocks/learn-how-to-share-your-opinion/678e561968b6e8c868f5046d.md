# --explanation--

`Since` hutumika kuanzisha sababu ya jambo fulani kutokea. Inaweza kueleweka kama "kwa sababu". Kwa mfano:

`Since it's late, the meeting has been canceled.` - Hapa, `since` hutumika kutoa sababu ya mkutano kufutwa.

`Significant` inamaanisha jambo muhimu au lenye athari kubwa. Kwa mfano:

`The new security feature had a significant impact on reducing system vulnerabilities.` - Hii inamaanisha kwamba kipengele cha usalama kilicheza jukumu muhimu katika kuboresha usalama wa mfumo.
