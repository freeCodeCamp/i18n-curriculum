# --description--

`To skim` 是指快速閱讀或瀏覽某物，以獲取主要概念而不專注於細節。 例如，`She skimmed the article to understand the general topic.`

`To skim through` 類似，意思是快速瀏覽一段文字或文件。例如，`He skimmed through the manual to find the troubleshooting section.`
