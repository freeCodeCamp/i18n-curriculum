# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia kivumishi cha kiwango cha juu (superlative) kwa usahihi?  

## --answers--

`This laptop is more powerful than that one.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii inatumia kiwango cha kulinganisha (comparative) (`more powerful`), si kiwango cha juu (superlative).

---

`She is a very skilled developer in her team.`  

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii haitumii kiwango cha juu (superlative) kwa usahihi.

---

`This is the fastest processor on the market.`  

---

`This software is easier to use than the old version.`  

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii inatumia kiwango cha kulinganisha (comparative) (`easier`), lakini kiwango cha juu (superlative) kinalinganisha vitu vitatu au zaidi.

## --video-solution--

3  

# --explanation--

Kivumishi cha kiwango cha juu (superlative) kinalinganisha vitu vitatu au zaidi kuonyesha kiwango cha juu au cha chini kabisa.  

Ongeza `-est` kwa vivumishi vifupi (kama `fastest`), na tumia `most` kwa vivumishi virefu (kama `most efficient`). Baadhi ya vivumishi vina aina zisizo za kawaida (kama `best`). Hakikisha kutumia `the` kabla ya vivumishi vya kiwango cha juu.
