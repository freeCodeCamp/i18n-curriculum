# --explanation--

`How about` inatumika kupendekeza wakati au wazo. Neno `during` linatumika kubainisha kipindi cha wakati ambapo jambo litafanyika. Kwa mfano:

- `How about we meet at 3 PM?` - Hii inapendekeza wakati wa mkutano.

- `I will call you during the break.` - Hii inabainisha kuwa wito utafanyika wakati wa mapumziko.
