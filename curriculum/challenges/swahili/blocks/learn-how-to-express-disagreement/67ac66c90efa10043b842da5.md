- `I understand, but we don't have enough time to implement this feature.` - Msemaji anakubaliana lakini anaonyesha ukomo wa muda.

- `I understand, but the current design is more user-friendly.` - Msemaji anaona mtazamo mwingine lakini anaamini muundo wa sasa ni bora.
