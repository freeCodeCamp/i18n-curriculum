# --explanation--

`Typically` hutumika kuelezea jambo linalotokea kwa kawaida au kama inavyotarajiwa. Ni sawa na `usually` na mara nyingi hutumika kuzungumzia kile kinachotokea kawaida. Kwa mfano:

- `Typically, we have meetings on Mondays.` - Hii ina maana mikutano kawaida hufanyika Jumatatu.

- `She typically arrives at work by 9 AM.` - Hii ina maana yeye kawaida hufika kazini saa tatu asubuhi.
