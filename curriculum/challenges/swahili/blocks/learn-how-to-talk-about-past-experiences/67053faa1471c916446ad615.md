# --explanation--

`Something` hutumika katika sentensi chanya kuonyesha kwamba kitu kipo au kilitokea. Kwa mfano:  

- `He found something unusual in the logs.` - Aligundua kwamba kumbukumbu zilikuwa na kitu tofauti na kawaida.

- `She noticed something during the meeting.` - Kitu kimoja maalum kilimvutia wakati wa mkutano.

Kinyume chake, `anything` hutumika katika sentensi hasi au maswali. Kwa mfano:

- Hasi: `He didn't find anything in the logs.` - Kumbukumbu hazikumsaidia kuelewa tatizo.

- Swali: `Did she notice anything during the meeting?` - Unauliza kama aliweza kuona kitu wakati wa mkutano.

Katika sentensi hasi zenye `did not` au `didn't`, kitenzi kikuu hubaki katika umbo lake la msingi (`find`, `notice` nk), na `anything` hutumika badala ya `something`.

Hapa, James anasema, `That's when I noticed something odd`, ikimaanisha alipata kitu wakati wa mchakato wake wa ukaguzi.
