# --questions--

## --text--

Linda alichapisha sasisho kuhusu mchakato wa ukuzaji wa timu yake:

`Over the past year, we've been focusing on user feedback more than ever, and it's really changed our approach to development. Instead of just tackling technical issues, we're now looking at everything from the user's perspective.`

`Last month, we rolled out a major update inspired by user feedback, and the response has been amazing. Now, rather than just reacting to issues, we're proactively making improvements based on what our users tell us.`

`Feedback has truly become central to our work, and we're excited to keep this user-centered approach at the heart of our development process.`

Nini kimebadilika katika mbinu ya ukuzaji ya timu hiyo katika mwaka uliopita?

## --answers--

Wameacha kufanya masasisho kwenye jukwaa.

### --feedback--

Linda anataja kuwa wanaji `making improvements` kwa bidii, jambo linaloonyesha kuwa hawajakuacha kufanya masasisho.

---

Sasa wanazingatia tu kutatua matatizo ya kiufundi.

### --feedback--

Linda anaelezea mabadiliko kutoka `tackling technical issues` kwenda makini zaidi na maoni ya watumiaji, badala ya kushughulikia matatizo ya kiufundi pekee.

---

Hawazingatii tena mtazamo wa mtumiaji.

### --feedback--

Linda anasema kuwa `user feedback has become central to our work`, jambo linaloonyesha kuwa kwa kweli wanapeleka kipaumbele mtazamo wa mtumiaji.

---

Wanaweka kipaumbele maoni ya watumiaji katika mchakato wao wa ukuzaji.

4

# --explanation--

Ili kupata jibu sahihi, zingatia sehemu ambapo Linda anaelezea mbinu yao ya sasa kuhusiana na maoni ya watumiaji.

Katika sentensi `user feedback has become essential to our work`, Linda anasisitiza kuwa maoni sasa ni kipaumbele. Analinganisha hili na makini yao ya zamani kwenye `just tackling technical issues`, kuonyesha kuwa mbinu yao imebadilika kuwa ni pamoja na maboresho ya makini yanayomlenga mtumiaji.

Kwa kutambua misemo hii muhimu, unaweza kuona kuwa kuweka kipaumbele maoni ya watumiaji ndio mabadiliko makuu katika mbinu yao ya ukuzaji katika mwaka uliopita.
