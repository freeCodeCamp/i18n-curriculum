# --description--

Wakati `while` inapotumika mwanzoni mwa sentensi, huanzisha wazo au mtazamo tofauti. Kwa mfano, `While I enjoy playing sports, I still spend most of my free time reading.` Inaonyesha tofauti kati ya kufurahia michezo na jinsi muda unavyotumika kweli.

Neno `still` hutumika kusisitiza kwamba msemaji anashikilia maoni yake. Kama ilivyo katika `While the weather is cold, I still prefer to walk to work.`, inaonyesha kwamba hata akiwa na baridi, msemaji huchagua kutembea.
