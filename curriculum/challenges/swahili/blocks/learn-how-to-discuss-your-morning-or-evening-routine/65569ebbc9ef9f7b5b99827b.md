# --description--

`It seems like...` hutumika kushiriki hisia au makadirio kuhusu jambo fulani, kawaida kwa msingi wa kile unachoona au kugundua. Kwa mfano:

`It seems like it's cold outside!` - Hii ina maana hujaja bado, lakini unaona watu wamevaa koti, hivyo unadhani lazima iwe baridi.

Unaweza pia kusema, `It seems like it's going to rain`, ikiwa unaona mawingu meusi, au `It seems like you're happy today`, ikiwa mtu anacheka sana. Usemi huu ni muhimu unapotaka kueleza jinsi jambo linavyoonekana au kuhisi kwako.
