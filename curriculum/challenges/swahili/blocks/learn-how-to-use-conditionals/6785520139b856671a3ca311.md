# --explanation--

Neno `some` linamaanisha `a part of` au `not all`. Kwa mfano:

- `Some of the team members are working remotely.` - Sio wanachama wote wa timu, ni sehemu tu yao.

- `She bought some new books to prepare for the exam.` - Vitabu vichache, si vitabu vyote vilivyopo.

Kutumia `some` kunaonyesha kwamba ingawa TechCorp ni muhimu kwa kampuni ndogo ndogo, haifadhili miradi yao yote, bali sehemu tu. Hii ndiyo sababu kupoteza TechCorp bado kunaweza kuwa na athari kubwa.
