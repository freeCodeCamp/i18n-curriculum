# --explanation--

Sentensi inatumia `First Conditional` kuelezea hali inayowezekana sasa au baadaye. Kwa mfano:

`If you restart the system, the problem will go away.` - Kuanza upya mfumo kunaweza kutatua tatizo.

Katika mazungumzo, `If it's not functioning` inarejelea hali, na `the operating system won't load` inaelezea matokeo ya hali hiyo.
