# --explanation--

Sentensi hii inatumia `First Conditional` kuelezea uhusiano wa sababu na matokeo.

Usemi `if there's a software crash` unaelezea hali. Matokeo, `it can be because of incompatible drivers or outdated software`, yanaonyesha sababu inayowezekana ya kuanguka kwa programu.

Vifungu vikuu katika sentensi za masharti mara nyingi hutumia vitenzi vya modal, kama `can`, `could`, `may`, `should`, n.k. Kwa mfano:

- `If the system overheats, it can shut down automatically.` - Joto kupita kiasi kila mara lina uwezekano wa kusababisha mfumo kuzimwa.

- `If users don't follow instructions, errors can occur.` - Kutofuata miongozo kila mara kunaunda uwezekano wa makosa.
