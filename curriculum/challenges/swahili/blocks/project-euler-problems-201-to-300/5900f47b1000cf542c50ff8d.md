# --description--

Kwa nambari chanya $n$, fafanua $S(n)$ kama jumla ya nambari kamili $x$, ambazo $1 < x < n$ na $x^3 \equiv 1\bmod n$.

Wakati $n = 91$, kuna thamani 8 zinazowezekana za $x$, yaani: 9, 16, 22, 29, 53, 74, 79, 81. Hivyo, $S(91) = 9 + 16 + 22 + 29 + 53 + 74 + 79 + 81 = 363$.

Tafuta $S(13\\,082\\,761\\,331\\,670\\,030)$.
