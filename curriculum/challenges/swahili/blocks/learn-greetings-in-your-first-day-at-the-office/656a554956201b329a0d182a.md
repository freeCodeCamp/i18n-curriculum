# --description--

Unapozungumzia vifaa au zana, `this` na `that` husaidia kuonyesha ni ipi.

# --questions--

## --text--

Chagua sentensi inayotumia `this` na `that` kwa usahihi.

## --answers--

`This mouse here works well, but that one over there doesn't.`

---

`That is my phone on this table, but this is my charger in that room.`

### --feedback--

Muundo wa sentensi ni mgumu kueleweka. Kawaida, `this` na `that` hazitumiki kwa njia hii, na maneno `room` na `table` hufanya mahali pawe na mkanganyiko.

---

`That are the wires we need.`

### --feedback--

`Wires` ni wingi, kwa hivyo fomu sahihi inapaswa kuwa `these are`, si `that are`.

---

`This app on my phone, and that games on my computer.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi haijakamilika. Zaidi ya hayo, `games` ni wingi, kwa hivyo `those games` ingekuwa sahihi.
