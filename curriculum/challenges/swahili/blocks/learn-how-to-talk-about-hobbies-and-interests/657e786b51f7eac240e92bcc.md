# --description--

`Would you like...?` ni njia ya heshima ya kutoa kitu au kuuliza mtu kama anataka kitu.

Unapotaka kutoa kitu, unaweza kukieleza moja kwa moja baada ya usemi `would you like`. Kwa mfano, unaweza kuuliza `Would you like some coffee?` unapotaka kumtoa mgeni kahawa.

Ikiwa unapendekeza kufanya jambo fulani, kama `going shopping`, kumbuka kuweka `to` kati ya usemi na kitendo. Kwa mfano, `Would you like to go shopping?`

Mwishowe, ikiwa unauliza mtu kama unapaswa kufanya kitu kwa niaba yao, tumia `me` kati ya `would you like` na `to`. Kwa mfano, `Would you like me to make some coffee?`

# --questions--

## --text--

Ni katika sentensi gani `would you like` imetumika kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`Would you like to come to the movie with us?`

---

`Would you like I make dinner tonight?`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii si sahihi. Matumizi sahihi yangekuwa `Would you like me to make dinner tonight?`

---

`Would you likes a glass of water?`

### --feedback--

Katika muktadha huu, `like` haihitaji kumalizika na `-s`. Fomu sahihi ni `Would you like a glass of water?`

---

`Would you like go to the park?`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii inakosa `to` kabla ya `go`. Sentensi sahihi inapaswa kuwa `Would you like to go to the park?`
