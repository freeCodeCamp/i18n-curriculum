`If that happened, a lot of smaller companies were be in trouble, too.`

### --feedback--

Kwa hali ya masharti, ikiwa sharti liko katika wakati wa `Past Simple` (dhana), matokeo yanapaswa kutumia `would`.

---

`If that happened, a lot of smaller companies would be in trouble, too.`

---

`If that happened, a lot of smaller companies will be in trouble, too.`

### --feedback--

Kutumia `will` si sahihi katika `Second Conditional`. Matokeo ya sharti la dhana yanapaswa kutumia `would`.

---

`If that happens, a lot of smaller companies will be in trouble, too.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii inatumia `First Conditional`, ambayo ni kwa nafasi halisi zinazowezekana. Hata hivyo, sentensi iliyotolewa ni hali ya dhana, na hivyo ni `Second Conditional`.

# --explanation--

Sentensi sahihi, `If that happened, a lot of smaller companies would be in trouble, too`, ni `Second Conditional`, inayotumika kwa hali za dhana au zisizo halisi katika sasa au baadaye. Katika sentensi hii:

- `If that happened` ni sharti la dhana.

- `A lot of smaller companies would be in trouble, too` ni matokeo yaliyofikirika.
