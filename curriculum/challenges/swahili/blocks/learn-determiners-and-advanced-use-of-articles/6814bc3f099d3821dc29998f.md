# --explanation--

Unapozungumzia wazo au dhana ya dhahania kwa mara ya kwanza, unatumia `a` — kama `a good option`. Husaidia kuanzisha kitu cha jumla na kisichoeleweka. Mifano zaidi:

- `That's a helpful suggestion!`

- `That's a good idea!`
