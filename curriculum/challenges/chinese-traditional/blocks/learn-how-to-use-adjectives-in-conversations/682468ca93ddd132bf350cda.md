# --explanation--

`Fix` means to correct or repair something that was broken or not working. Its past form is `fixed`. 例如：

`We fixed the login error.` – The problem with logging in is now solved.

`Many of` is used to refer to a large number taken from a specific group. 例如：

`Many of the bugs were found during testing.` – A large number of bugs (not all) were discovered.

`From before` refers to something that happened in the past. 例如：

`I remember this issue from before.` – It is something that happened earlier in time.
