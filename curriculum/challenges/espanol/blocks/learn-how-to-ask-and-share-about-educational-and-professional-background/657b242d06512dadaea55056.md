# --description--

To `attend` means to be present at an event or place. A menudo se usa con respecto a escuelas, universidades, reuniones o eventos. Implica participación regular o presencia en un lugar o evento específico. For example:

`Many developers attended the coding workshop.` - This means the developers were present at the workshop.

# --questions--

## --text--

Si un amigo te dice, `I'm going to attend the new art workshop`, ¿qué están planeando hacer?

## --answers--

They are planning to teach at the art workshop.

### --feedback--

`Attend` sugiere participar como estudiante o miembro, no como maestro.

---

They might visit the workshop once.

### --feedback--

`Attend` implica participación regular o programada, no una visita única.

---

They are not interested in art workshops.

### --feedback--

`Attend` indicates interest and intent to participate, not a lack of interest.
