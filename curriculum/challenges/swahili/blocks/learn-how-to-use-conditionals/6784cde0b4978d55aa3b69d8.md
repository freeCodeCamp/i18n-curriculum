## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayofuata sarufi kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`If TechCorp goes out of business, it would be a big shock.`

### --feedback--

Hii inachanganya nyakati kwa njia isiyo sahihi. Kwa hali ya nadharia, tumia wakati wa `Past Simple` katika sharti na `would` katika matokeo.

---

`If TechCorp went out of business, it would be a big shock.`

---

`If TechCorp went out of business, it will be a big shock.`

### --feedback--

Kutumia `will` si sahihi kwa `Second Conditional`. Unapaswa kutumia `would` kwa hali zinazofikirika au zisizowezekana.

---

`If TechCorp goes out of business, it will be a big shock.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii inatumia `First Conditional`, ambayo ni kwa uwezekano halisi, lakini Tom na Lisa wanazungumzia hali ya nadharia.

# --explanation--

Sentensi sahihi, `If TechCorp went out of business, it would be a big shock`, inafuata muundo wa `Second Conditional`:

`If` + wakati wa `Past Simple`, `would` + kitenzi cha msingi.

Muundo huu hutumika kwa hali zisizo halisi au zisizowezekana, kama kufikiria TechCorp ikifungwa biashara.
