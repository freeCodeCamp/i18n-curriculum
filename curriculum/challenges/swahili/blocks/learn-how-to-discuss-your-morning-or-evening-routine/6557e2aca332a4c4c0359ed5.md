# --description--

`As well` ni usemi unaotumika kuongeza kitu kwa kile kilichosemwa tu, kama `also` au `too`. Mara nyingi hutumika kuhusisha kitu au mtu mwingine katika tendo au kauli. Kwa mfano:

`I'll have coffee and a muffin as well.` - Hautapata tu kahawa, bali pia muffin.
