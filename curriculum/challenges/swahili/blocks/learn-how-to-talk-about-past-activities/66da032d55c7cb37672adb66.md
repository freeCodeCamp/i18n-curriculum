# --questions--

## --text--

Ni chaguo gani kinachoonyesha kuwa mauzo yalipungua sana?

## --answers--

`There has been a significant drop in sales this quarter.`

---

`There has been a significant increase in sales this quarter.`

### --feedback--

`Increase` ina maana kiasi kimeongezeka, si kupungua.

---

`There has been a significant rise in sales this quarter.`

### --feedback--

`Rise` inaonyesha mwelekeo wa kuongezeka, unaoashiria ukuaji wa mauzo, si kupungua.

---

`There has been a moderate drop in sales this quarter.`

### --feedback--

Ingawa sentensi hii inataja `drop`, neno `moderate` lina maana ya kupungua kidogo au si kubwa sana, si kupungua `significant`.

1

# --explanation--

`Drop` inahusu kupungua kwa kiasi au kiwango. Watu wanaposema kuna `a drop in sales`, ina maana mauzo yamepungua.

Unapoongeza neno `significant` kabla ya `drop`, ina maana kupungua ni kubwa au muhimu. Kwa mfano:

`A significant drop in sales` - Mauzo yamepungua sana.
