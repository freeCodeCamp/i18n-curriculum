# --description--

Huwezi kutumia `didn't` na baadhi ya vitenzi kuunda sentensi hasi katika wakati uliopita. Mfano mmoja ni kitenzi `to be`.

Ikiwa unataka kuunda sentensi hasi ukitumia kitenzi `to be` katika wakati uliopita, ongeza tu `not` baada ya `was` au `were`. Kwa mfano:

| Chanya | Hasi |
| --- | --- |
| `It was working normally.` | `It wasn't working normally.` |
| `They were informed earlier.` | `They weren't informed earlier.` |

`Wasn't` ni kifupisho cha `was not` na `weren't` ni kifupisho cha `were not`.
