`He shared the layout of the new office.`－這表示物品擺放的位置規劃。

`She wrote the first draft of the proposal.`－仍然可以編輯。

`I'll send it over once it's ready.`－這表示分享一個檔案或訊息。

`She gave the file to her manager.` – 它顯示誰接收它。
