`Team meeting` ni wakati uliopangwa ambapo wenzako hukutana ili kujadili kazi. Kwa mfano, `We have a team meeting every Monday morning`.

Kusema `discuss` kunamaanisha kuzungumzia jambo kwa undani. Kwa mfano, `We discuss project updates during the meeting`.
