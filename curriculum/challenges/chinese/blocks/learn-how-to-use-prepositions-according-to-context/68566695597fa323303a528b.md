# --questions--

## --text--

哪个句子正确使用了单词 `during` 来表示时间？

## --answers--

`He arrived during the door.`

### --feedback--

`During` 应该参考一个时间段，而不是一个对象。

---

`She walked during the park.`

### --feedback--

`During` 不应与这样的地点一起使用。

---

`We had several meetings during the afternoon.`

---

`They spoke during each other loudly.`

### --feedback--

听起来意思应该是 `to` 或 `with`。

## --video-solution--

3

# --explanation--

`During` 用于表示某事发生在特定时间段内。它后面跟随时间词，如 `the afternoon`、`the meeting` 或 `the week`。

在句子 `We had several meetings during the afternoon` 中，它的意思是会议发生在下午时间段的某个时刻。
