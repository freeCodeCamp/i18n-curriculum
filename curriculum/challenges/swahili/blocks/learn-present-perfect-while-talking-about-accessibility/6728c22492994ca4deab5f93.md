## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia `for` na `since` kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`I have been working here since three years.`

### --feedback--

`Since` inapaswa kutumika kwa wakati maalum, si kwa muda mrefu kama `three years`.

---

`I have been studying for the exam since last week.`

---

`I will focus on this project for tomorrow.`

### --feedback--

`For` inapaswa kuonyesha muda wa muda, si wakati maalum wa baadaye kama `tomorrow`.

---

`She has lived in this city for 2010.`

### --feedback--

`Since` inapaswa kutumika kuonyesha mwaka maalum (mfano, `since 2010`), wakati `for` inaonyesha muda wa muda.
