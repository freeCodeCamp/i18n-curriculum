# --description--

Kwa mviringo wowote $T$ kwenye ndege, inaweza kuonyeshwa kuwa kuna ellipse moja tu yenye eneo kubwa kabisa ambayo iko kabisa ndani ya $T$.

<img alt="ellipse completely inside a triangle" src="https://cdn.freecodecamp.org/curriculum/project-euler/ellipses-inside-triangles.png" style="background-color: white; padding: 10px; display: block; margin-right: auto; margin-left: auto; margin-bottom: 1.2rem;">

Kwa nambari fulani $n$, zingatia mviringo $T$ zenye:

-   ncha za $T$ zikiwa na kuratibu za nambari kamili zenye thamani kamili isiyozidi $n$, na
-   foci<sup>1</sup> za ellipse yenye eneo kubwa kabisa ndani ya $T$ ni $(\sqrt{13}, 0)$ na $(-\sqrt{13}, 0)$.

Acha $A(n)$ iwe jumla ya maeneo ya mviringo yote kama hayo.

Kwa mfano, ikiwa $n = 8$, kuna mviringo miwili kama hiyo. Ncha zao ni (-4,-3), (-4,3), (8,0) na (4,3), (4,-3), (-8,0), na eneo la kila mviringo ni 36. Hivyo basi $A(8) = 36 + 36 = 72$.

Inaweza kuthibitishwa kuwa $A(10) = 252$, $A(100) = 34\\,632$ na $A(1000) = 3\\,529\\,008$.

Tafuta $A(1\\,000\\,000\\,000)$.

<sup>1</sup>Foci (wingi wa focus) wa ellipse ni pointi mbili $A$ na $B$ ambapo kwa kila pointi $P$ kwenye ukingo wa ellipse, $AP + PB$ ni thabiti.
