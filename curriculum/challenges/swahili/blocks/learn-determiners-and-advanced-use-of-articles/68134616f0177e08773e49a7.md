### --feedback--

Usemi huu wa maneno mawili unamaanisha kualika au kuongeza mtu mpya katika timu au mradi. Neno la kwanza linaishia na `-ing`.

# --explanation--

`Bring in` ni kitenzi cha maneno kinachomaanisha kualika, kuongeza, au kuajiri mtu kujiunga na timu, mradi, au kampuni. Kwa mfano:

- `He brought in a security expert to check the system.` - Aliomba mtaalamu ajiunge na kuchunguza usalama.

- `We might need to bring in outside help if the issue continues.` - Huenda wakahitaji msaada wa nje ikiwa tatizo halitakoma.
