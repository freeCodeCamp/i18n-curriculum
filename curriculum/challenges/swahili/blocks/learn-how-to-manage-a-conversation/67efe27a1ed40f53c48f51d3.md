# --explanation--

`To take long` ina maana kwamba jambo fulani linahitaji muda zaidi kuliko lilivyopangwa au kutegemewa awali. Inatumika kuzungumzia muda wa jambo fulani. Unaposema jambo ni `longer than expected`, unasisitiza kwamba linachukua muda zaidi kuliko lilivyotarajiwa. Aina ya kulinganisha ya `long` (`longer`) hutumika kulinganisha muda halisi uliotumika na ule uliotarajiwa au kupangwa. Kwa mfano:


- `The delivery took longer than planned.` – Hii ina maana kwamba utoaji ulahitaji muda zaidi kuliko ulivyotarajiwa awali.

- `The journey took longer than expected due to traffic.` – Hii ina maana kwamba safari ilidumu muda zaidi kuliko walivyodhani itadumu.
