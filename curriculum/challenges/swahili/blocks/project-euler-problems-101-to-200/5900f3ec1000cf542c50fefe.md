# --description--

Radikali ya $n$, $rad(n)$, ni bidhaa ya gawanyio msingi tofauti za $n$. Kwa mfano, $504 = 2^3 × 3^2 × 7$, hivyo $rad(504) = 2 × 3 × 7 = 42$.

Tutaeleza triplet ya nambari kamili chanya (a, b, c) kuwa abc-hit ikiwa:

1. $GCD(a, b) = GCD(a, c) = GCD(b, c) = 1$
2. $a &lt; b$
3. $a + b = c$
4. $rad(abc) &lt; c$

Kwa mfano, (5, 27, 32) ni abc-hit, kwa sababu:

1. $GCD(5, 27) = GCD(5, 32) = GCD(27, 32) = 1$
2. $5 &lt; 27$
3. $5 + 27 = 32$
4. $rad(4320) = 30 &lt; 32$

Inajitokeza kuwa abc-hits ni nadra sana na kuna abc-hits thelathini na moja tu kwa $c &lt; 1000$, na $\sum{c} = 12523$.

Tafuta $\sum{c}$ kwa $c &lt; 120000$.
