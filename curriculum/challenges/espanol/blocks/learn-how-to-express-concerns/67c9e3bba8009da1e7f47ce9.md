# --explanation--

María usa **discurso indirecto** después de `they said` para compartir lo que el equipo declaró anteriormente. En el discurso indirecto, los verbos a menudo cambian a una forma pasada, pero `Present Continuous` (`are putting`) permanece en continuo (`they're already putting in extra hours`). Por ejemplo:

- Discurso directo: `"We are working late," they said.`

- Estilo indirecto: `They said they were working late.`

Aquí tienes otro ejemplo:

- Discurso directo: `"I have finished the report," she said.`

- Discurso indirecto: `She said she had finished the report.`
