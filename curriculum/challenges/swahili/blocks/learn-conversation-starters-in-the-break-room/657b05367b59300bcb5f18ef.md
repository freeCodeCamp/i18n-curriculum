# --description--

Unaweza kutumia kitenzi `to be` kuuliza maswali kuhusu hisia, hali, au masharti. Unapotaka kuuliza maswali kama haya, unabadilisha tu nafasi za mhusika (kama `you` au `he`) na kitenzi `to be` (kama `are` au `is`).

Kwa mfano, kubadilisha sentensi hii `You are a developer.` kuwa swali, unabadili `you` na `are`: `Are you a developer?` Angalia jinsi mpangilio unavyobadilika kuuliza swali.

Hapa kuna mifano zaidi ya kubadilisha kauli kuwa maswali kwa kutumia `to be`: 

| Thibitisho | Swali |
| --- | --- |
| `He is here.` | `Is he here?` |
| `They are ready.` | `Are they ready?` |

# --instructions--

Sikiliza sauti na ukamilishe sentensi hapa chini.
