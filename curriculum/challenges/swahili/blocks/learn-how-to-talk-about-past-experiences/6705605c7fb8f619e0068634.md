# --explanation--

`Issue` katika muktadha huu inamaanisha tatizo au ugumu unaohitaji kutatuliwa. Katika sentensi, inahusu vyeti vya kuingia kwenye hifadhidata kuwa si sahihi. Kwa mfano:  

- `There was an issue with the system configuration.` - Tatizo lilihusiana na usanidi wa mfumo.

- `The main issue was a missing file.` - Tatizo lilisababishwa na faili kuwa mahali pasipo sahihi.

`Them` ni kitenzi cha kurejelea nomino nyingi zilizotajwa awali. Katika swali la Lisa, `them` inarejelea `credentials`. Kwa mfano:

- `The files were corrupted. Did you fix them?` - `them` inarejelea mafaili.

- `The permissions were incorrect. I updated them this morning.` - `them` inarejelea ruhusa.

Swali la Lisa linauliza kama James alisahihisha vyeti vya kuingia.
