# --description--

Acha $P(m,n)$ iwe idadi ya maneno tofauti katika jedwali la kuzidisha la $m×n$.

Kwa mfano, jedwali la kuzidisha la 3×4 linaonekana hivi:

$$\begin{array}{c}
  ×          & \mathbf{1} & \mathbf{2} & \mathbf{3} & \mathbf{4}  \\\\
  \mathbf{1} & 1          & 2          & 3          & 4  \\\\
  \mathbf{2} & 2          & 4          & 6          & 8  \\\\
  \mathbf{3} & 3          & 6          & 9          & 12
\end{array}$$

Kuna maneno 8 tofauti {1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 9, 12}, kwa hiyo $P(3, 4) = 8$.

Umepatiwa kwamba:

$$\begin{align}
  & P(64, 64) = 1\\,263, \\\\
  & P(12, 345) = 1\\,998, \text{ na} \\\\
  & P(32, {10}^{15}) = 13\\,826\\,382\\,602\\,124\\,302. \\\\
\end{align}$$

Tafuta $P(64, {10}^{16})$.
