## --text--

哪个句子正确使用了短语 `a lot of`？

## --answers--

`She learned a lot of programming languages.`

---

`He has a lot of careful in his work.`

### --feedback--

单词 `careful` 是形容词，不是名词。`A lot of` 应该与名词一起使用。

---

`I have  a lot of idea for the new app.`

### --feedback--

可数名词 `idea` 在与短语 `a lot of` 连用时应使用复数形式。

---

`She has a lot of the most experience in our team.`

### --feedback--

比较品质时，应使用 `more` 或 `most`，而不是 `a lot of`。
