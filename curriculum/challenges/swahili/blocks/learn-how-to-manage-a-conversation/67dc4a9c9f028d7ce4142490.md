# --explanation--

Majibu mafupi kama `It does` hutumika mara nyingi kuthibitisha jambo bila kurudia sentensi kamili au kutumia `yes` au `no`. Badala ya kusema `Yes, it works for me` au `Yes, it does` tu, jibu hupunguzwa kuwa `It does`. Mfano mwingine:

- **Mark:** `Does this solve the problem?` - Mtu huyu anauliza kama jambo fulani ni suluhisho nzuri.

- **Bob:** `It does.` - Hii inathibitisha kuwa suluhisho linafanya kazi bila kurudia sentensi yote.
