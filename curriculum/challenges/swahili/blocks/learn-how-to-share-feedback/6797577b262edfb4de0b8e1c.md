# --explanation--

Muundo `how + comparative adjective` hutumika kusisitiza au kutathmini kiwango au upana wa jambo fulani ikilinganishwa na lingine. Kwa mfano:  

- `I see how better documentation could help the team.` - Hii inaonyesha Jessica anaelewa faida za nyaraka zilizo bora zaidi.

- `He realized how faster responses could improve customer satisfaction.` - Hii inasisitiza athari za majibu ya haraka.

Kitenzi `focus` kina maana ya kutoa makini au juhudi kwa jambo fulani. Kwa mfano:

`She is focusing on learning new skills for her career.` - Hii ina maana anapeleka makini yake katika kuboresha ujuzi wake.
