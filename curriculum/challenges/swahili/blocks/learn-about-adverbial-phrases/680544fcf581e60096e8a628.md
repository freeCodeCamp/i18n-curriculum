# --explanation--

Muundo `as` + kivumishi + `as possible` hutumika kulinganisha au kusisitiza kiwango cha kitu fulani. Ni muhimu wakati wa kuweka malengo au kuelezea hali bora.

`We want the launch to be as smooth as possible.` – Hii ina maana wanataka uzinduzi uende kwa urahisi sana, bila matatizo yoyote.
