# --description--

`Must` na `have to` dhihirisha wajibu. `Must` mara nyingi hutumika kwa wajibu mkali wa kibinafsi au wa ndani, wakati `have to` ni zaidi kwa mahitaji ya nje au sheria. Kwa mfano:

- `I must finish this report because it's important to me.` - Hapa, wajibu unatokana na hisia au uamuzi wa msemaji mwenyewe. Mtu huchagua kuufanya muhimu.

- `I have to finish this report by 5 PM because my boss said so.` - Hapa, wajibu unatokana na sheria au mamlaka ya nje. Bosi huweka tarehe ya mwisho, hivyo si uamuzi wa kibinafsi wa msemaji.

Tofauti hii husaidia kuonyesha kama wajibu ni wa ndani (kutoka kwako mwenyewe) au wa nje (kutoka nje, kama sheria au maelekezo).

### --feedback--

Sheria za kampuni zinaonyesha wajibu wa nje, unaofaa zaidi kwa `have to`.

`I have to make sure the security cameras work because I believe it's crucial.`

### --feedback--

Imani binafsi kuhusu umuhimu inaendana zaidi na `must`.

---

`I must avoid sharing my password with other people as part of the company's security policy.`

### --feedback--

Hii ni sharti kutoka kampuni, na inaendana zaidi na `have to`.

---

`I have to check the security cameras as part of my job duties.`
