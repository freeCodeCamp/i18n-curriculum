# --description--

Neno `never` ni kielezi kinachotumika kumaanisha "sio wakati wowote" au "sio kamwe". Mara nyingi hutumika kuzungumzia mambo ambayo mtu hafanyi au hajawahi kufanya. Kwa mfano:

- `I have never been to Australia.` - Hii ina maana mtu huyo hajawahi kutembelea Australia wakati wowote.

- `She never eats fast food.` - Hii ina maana yeye hakuli chakula cha haraka wakati wowote.

Neno `thought` ni wakati uliopita wa `think`. `Thought of that` ina maana ya kuwa amefikiria au kuja na wazo hapo awali. Kwa mfano:

`I thought of going to the beach, but it was too cold.` - Hii ina maana mtu huyo alifikiria kwenda pwani.
