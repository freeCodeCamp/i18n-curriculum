# --explanation--

`Neither ... nor` ni muundo wa viongezi vinavyofanana vinavyotumika kuonyesha kwamba vitu viwili vyote si kweli. Kwa mfano:

- `Neither the app nor the website is working right now.` - Programu na tovuti zote mbili hazifanyi kazi.

- `He has neither the experience nor the skills for this task.` - Hana uzoefu wala ujuzi.

Tumia `the` kabla ya `developers` na `project managers` kwa sababu vinahusu majukumu maalum ndani ya timu.
