# --description--

`Anyone who` 用來指涉符合特定條件或需求的任何人。 

在技術領域中，它經常用來描述具有特定技能或知識的人。 例如，`Anyone who understands JavaScript can modify this script.`

# --questions--

## --text--

哪一句話在技術背景關係中正確使用了 `Anyone who`？

## --answers--

`Anyone who has access to the server can upload the files.`

---

`Anyone who please fix this bug.`

### --feedback--

語法正確的用法是，`Anyone who knows the solution, please fix this bug.`

---

`Anyone who the programmer is busy now.`

### --feedback--

這句話語法不正確。較好的句子是，`Anyone who is a programmer is busy now.`

---

`Who anyone can join the meeting is not clear.`

### --feedback--

這句話的結構不正確。正確的表單應該是，`It is not clear who can join the meeting.`
