# --questions--

## --text--

你正在向你的經理報告新的辦公室佈局。哪一句正確描述了某個部門或房間的位置？

## --answers--

`The break room is between the IT department and the kitchen.`

---

`The meeting room is between of the lobby and the copy area.`

### --feedback--

`Between of` 不是正確的片語。應該只用 `between the lobby and the copy area`。

---

`Reception is between the sales team with the HR office.`

### --feedback--

你應該使用 `between ... and ...`，而不是 `between ... with ...`。

---

`The server room is in between to the hallway and the printer area.`

### --feedback--

它應該是在 `between the hallway and the printer area` 而非 `to`。

## --video-solution--

1

# --explanation--

`Between ... and ...` 用來描述位於兩個特定地點中間的位置。

正確的句子正確地遵循此結構：`between the IT department and the kitchen`。

其他選項誤用介系詞或連接詞，導致語法錯誤。
