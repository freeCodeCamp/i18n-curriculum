## --text--

Quale frase usa correttamente `don't` in una domanda tag?

## --answers--

`She likes coffee, don't she?`

### --feedback--

Il soggetto `she` richiede `doesn't`, non `don't`.

---

`We need to resolve this quickly, don't we?`

---

`They know the answer, don't it?`

### --feedback--

Il soggetto `they` richiede `don't they`, non `don't it`.

---

`The system is working fine, don't it?`

### --feedback--

Il soggetto `it` richiede `doesn't`, non `don't`.
