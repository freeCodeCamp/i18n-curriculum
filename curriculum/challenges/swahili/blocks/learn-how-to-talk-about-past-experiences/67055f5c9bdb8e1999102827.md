# --explanation--

Unapozungumza kuhusu kitu ambacho tayari umezitaja hapo awali, unaweza kutumia `it` kuibadilisha ikiwa ni nomino ya wingi au `them` ikiwa ni nomino ya wingi ili kuepuka kurudia. Kwa mfano:

- `You were looking for a car to buy. Did you buy it?` - `It` hutumika badala ya `the car` katika swali.

- `How were your tests last week? Did you do well on all of them?` - `Them` hubadilisha `your tests` hapa.
