# --description--

Maneno `absolutely` na `definitely` yanaonyesha kwa nguvu uhakika na dhamira. `Absolutely` hutumika kutoa ndiyo yenye msisitizo au kukubaliana, wakati `definitely` inamaanisha 'bila shaka yoyote,' ikithibitisha dhamira au uhakika wa msemaji. Kwa mfano, ikiwa utaulizwa, `Are you sure you can complete this project?` jibu la kujiamini linaweza kuwa, `Absolutely, I can definitely finish it on time.`
