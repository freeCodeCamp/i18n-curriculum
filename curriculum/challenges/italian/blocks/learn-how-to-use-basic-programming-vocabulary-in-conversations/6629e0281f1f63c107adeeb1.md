## --text--

Quale delle seguenti frasi usa correttamente il tempo Present Perfect Continuous?

## --answers--

`She has visited her grandmother.`

### --feedback--

Questa frase usa il Present Perfect, non il Present Perfect Continuous.

---

`They have been eat dinner.`

### --feedback--

La forma verbale corretta dovrebbe essere `eating`.

---

`We have been studying for the exam since January.`

---

`He has been plays soccer every Thursday.`

### --feedback--

Questa frase usa una forma verbale scorretta. La forma corretta dovrebbe essere `playing`.
