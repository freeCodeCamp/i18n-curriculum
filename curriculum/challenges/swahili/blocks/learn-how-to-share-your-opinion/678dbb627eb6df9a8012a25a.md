# --explanation--

`Development` linahusu mchakato wa kuunda, kubuni, na kudumisha programu. Pia linaweza kumaanisha kuboresha mfumo au programu. Kwa mfano:

`The development of the new app took six months.` - Sentensi hii ina maana kwamba mchakato wa kuunda na kujenga programu mpya ulidumu miezi sita.

`Environment` linahusu zana na mazingira ambapo zoezi, kama vile kuandika msimbo au kupima, hufanyika. Kwa mfano:

`The testing environment includes a computer with specific software to check the app's performance.` - Hii ina maana mazingira yameundwa kwa zana na hali maalum kusaidia kupima jinsi programu inavyofanya kazi.
