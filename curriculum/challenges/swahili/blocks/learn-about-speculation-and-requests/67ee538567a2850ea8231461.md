# --explanation--

`What did I do wrong?` hutumika mtu anapotaka kuelewa kosa lake. Hutumika sana katika hali tofauti. Kwa mfano:

- `I got an error in my code. What did I do wrong?` - Msemaji anataka kujua ni kosa gani alilofanya katika msimbo wake.

- `My presentation didn't go well. What did I do wrong?` - Msemaji anatafuta njia za kuboresha.

Usemi huu ni muhimu kwa kujifunza kutokana na makosa na kuboresha katika hali tofauti. Mark anautumia kuelewa kosa lake.
