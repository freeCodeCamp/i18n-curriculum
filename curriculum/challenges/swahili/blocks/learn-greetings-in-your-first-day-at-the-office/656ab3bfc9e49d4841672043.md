# --description--

Wakati mtu anasema `too` mwishoni mwa sentensi, wanamaanisha `also` au `as well`. Kwa mfano, `I like it too` inamaanisha `I also like it`.

Kuongeza `too` mwishoni mwa kauli kunamaanisha kukubaliana au kuongeza msisitizo kwamba hali au ukweli sawa unahusiana na kinachojadiliwa.
