# --explanation--

`Grows with` inamaanisha kitu kinaendelea au kinapanuka sambamba na kitu kingine kwa muda. Kwa mfano:

- `This software grows with your company as it adds new employees.` – Hii inaonyesha programu inaweza kushughulikia watumizi zaidi wakati kampuni inapanuka.

- `Her skills grew with each new project.` – Hii inaonyesha kwamba alipoendelea na kazi zaidi, uwezo wake ulizidi kuimarika pia.
