# --explanation--

`After that` hutumika kuonyesha mpangilio wa matukio, ikimaanisha hatua au tukio linalofuata lilifuata kitu kilichotokea awali. Husaidia kupanga matendo kwa mpangilio wa wakati. Kwa mfano:  

- `We finished the code review. After that, we started debugging.` - Kwanza, walikagua msimbo. Kisha, walitafuta makosa.

- `He submitted the report. After that, he took a short break.` - Kwanza, alikamilisha ripoti. Kisha, alizuru.

`After that` ni sawa na maana na misemo kama `then` au `next`, lakini inasisitiza hasa kuwa kitendo kimoja kinafuata kingine kwa wakati. 

Hapa, James anatumia `after that` kuonyesha kuwa kutambua tatizo la nyaraka za hifadhidata kulikuja baada ya hatua nyingine katika mchakato wa kurekebisha makosa.
