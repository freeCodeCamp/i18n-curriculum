## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia neno `during` kwa usahihi kuzungumzia wakati?

## --answers--

`He arrived during the door.`

### --feedback--

`During` inapaswa kurejelea kipindi cha wakati, si kitu.

---

`She walked during the park.`

### --feedback--

`During` haipaswi kutumika na mahali kama huu.

---

`We had several meetings during the afternoon.`

---

`They spoke during each other loudly.`

### --feedback--

Inaonekana maana inapaswa kuwa `to` au `with`.

## --video-solution--

3

# --explanation--

`During` hutumika kuonyesha kwamba jambo linafanyika ndani ya kipindi maalum cha wakati. Hufuatiwa na neno la wakati kama `the afternoon`, `the meeting`, au `the week`.

Katika sentensi `We had several meetings during the afternoon`, inamaanisha mikutano ilifanyika wakati fulani ndani ya kipindi cha mchana.
