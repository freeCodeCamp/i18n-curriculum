# --description--

Chukulia nambari 3600. Ni ya kipekee sana, kwa sababu

$$\begin{align}
  & 3600 = {48}^2 + {36}^2   \\\\
  & 3600 = {20}^2 + {2×40}^2 \\\\
  & 3600 = {30}^2 + {3×30}^2 \\\\
  & 3600 = {45}^2 + {7×15}^2 \\\\
\end{align}$$

Vivyo hivyo, tunapata kwamba $88201 = {99}^2 + {280}^2 = {287}^2 + 2 × {54}^2 = {283}^2 + 3 × {52}^2 = {197}^2 + 7 × {84}^2$.

Mnamo mwaka 1747, Euler alithibitisha ni nambari gani zinazoweza kuwakilishwa kama jumla ya mraba miwili. Tunavutiwa na nambari $n$ ambazo zinakubali uwakilishi wa aina zote nne zifuatazo:

$$\begin{align}
  & n = {a_1}^2 + {b_1}^2  \\\\
  & n = {a_2}^2 + 2{b_2}^2 \\\\
  & n = {a_3}^2 + 3{b_3}^2 \\\\
  & n = {a_7}^2 + 7{b_7}^2 \\\\
\end{align}$$

ambapo $a_k$ na $b_k$ ni nambari kamili chanya.

Kuna nambari 75373 kama hizo ambazo hazizidi ${10}^7$.

Ni nambari ngapi kama hizo ambazo hazizidi $2 × {10}^9$?
