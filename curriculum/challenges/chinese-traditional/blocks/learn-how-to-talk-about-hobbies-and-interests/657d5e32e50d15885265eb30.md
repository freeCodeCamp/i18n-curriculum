# --description--

 In the Present Perfect Tense, you can contract `have` to `'ve` after `I`, `you`, `we` or `they`, and `has` to `'s` after `he`, `she` or `it`.

舉個例子：

- `I've walked to the park.`

- `She's seen the movie.`

# --questions--

## --text--
