## --text--

Which phrase means "very happy"?

## --answers--

`不高興 (bù gāo xìng)`

### --feedback--

---

`高興嗎 (gāo xìng ma)`

### --feedback--

---

`我高興 (wǒ gāo xìng)`

### --feedback--

## --video-solution--

2

# --explanation--

`很 (hěn)` means "very", and `高興 (gāo xìng)` means "happy". Together, `很高興 (hěn gāo xìng)` means "very happy".
