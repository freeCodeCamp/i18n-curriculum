# --explanation--

Katika usemi `limit what other parts can see`, `what` huanzisha kifungu cha nomino, ambacho hutumika kama kipengele cha `limit`.

Kifungu cha nomino ni kifungu kinachotumika kama nomino ndani ya sentensi. Kinaweza kufanya kazi kama kipengele au somo la sentensi. Kawaida huanza kwa maneno kama `that`, `what`, `whether`, `who`, `when`, `why`, `how`, n.k.

Kifungu cha nomino hutumia mpangilio wa kawaida wa maneno wa kauli (sio wa swali). Kwa mfano:

- `The team is checking why the system crashed during the update.` - `Why the system crashed during the update` ni kifungu cha nomino kinachotumika kama kipengele cha `checking`.
   
- `What the function returns depends on the input data.` - `What the function returns` ni kifungu cha nomino kinachotumika kama somo la `depends on`.
