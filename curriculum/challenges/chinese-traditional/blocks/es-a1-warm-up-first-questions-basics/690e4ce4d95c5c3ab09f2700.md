# --explanation--

字 `Encantada` 是女性講者用來表示「很高興認識你」的另一種常見說法。 

意思是「被迷住」或「高興」。

| Form | Gender | Speaker | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `Encantado` | Masculine (`-o`) | Male Speaker | Pleased to meet you. |
| `Encantada` | Feminine (`-a`) | Female Speaker | Pleased to meet you. |
