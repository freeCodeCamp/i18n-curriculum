`The company uses automation to schedule social media posts every day.`——这意味着使用软件工具来调度帖子，而无需每次手动输入。

`The team made a data-driven decision to improve website performance based on user activity data.` —— 这表明该决策是基于关于用户行为的实际数据，而非假设或猜测。
