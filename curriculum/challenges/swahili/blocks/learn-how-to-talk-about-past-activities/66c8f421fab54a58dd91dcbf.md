- `The company has offices across the country.` - Ofisi zimesambaa katika maeneo tofauti nchini.

- `She walked across the street.` - Alihama kutoka upande mmoja wa mtaa hadi mwingine.

`Across different screen sizes` ina maana kuwa tatizo linaonekana kwenye saizi nyingi tofauti za skrini.
