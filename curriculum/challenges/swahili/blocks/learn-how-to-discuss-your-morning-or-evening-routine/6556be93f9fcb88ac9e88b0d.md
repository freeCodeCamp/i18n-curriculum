# --description--

Usemi wa kihesabu wa methali ni misemo ambapo maneno pamoja yana maana tofauti na ile ambayo maneno binafsi yanaweza kuonyesha.

`A ton of energy` ni usemi wa kihesabu wa methali unaomaanisha kuwa na nguvu nyingi, sawa na kusema `loads of energy`.

Hapa kuna mifano zaidi ya misemo ya methali:

- `I slept like a rock.` - Hapa, `to sleep like a rock` inamaanisha kulala kwa usingizi mzito sana.
- `She's feeling blue today.` - Hapa, `to feel blue` inamaanisha kuhisi huzuni.
- `This math problem is a piece of cake.` - Hapa, `a piece of cake` inamaanisha kitu ni rahisi sana.
- `He's in hot water now.` - Hapa, `to be in hot water` inamaanisha kuwa katika shida.
- `They see eye to eye.` - Hapa, `to see eye to eye` inamaanisha kukubaliana na mtu.
