# --description--

Kwa seti yoyote $A$ ya nambari, acha $sum(A)$ iwe jumla ya vipengele vya $A$.

Tafakari seti $B = \\{1,3,6,8,10,11\\}$. Kuna seti ndogo 20 za $B$ zenye vipengele vitatu, na jumla zao ni:

$$\begin{align}
  & sum(\\{1,3,6\\}) = 10 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,3,8\\}) = 12 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,3,10\\}) = 14 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,3,11\\}) = 15 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,6,8\\}) = 15 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,6,10\\}) = 17 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,6,11\\}) = 18 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,8,10\\}) = 19 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,8,11\\}) = 20 \\\\
  & sum(\\{1,10,11\\}) = 22 \\\\
  & sum(\\{3,6,8\\}) = 17 \\\\
  & sum(\\{3,6,10\\}) = 19 \\\\
  & sum(\\{3,6,11\\}) = 20 \\\\
  & sum(\\{3,8,10\\}) = 21 \\\\
  & sum(\\{3,8,11\\}) = 22 \\\\
  & sum(\\{3,10,11\\}) = 24 \\\\
  & sum(\\{6,8,10\\}) = 24 \\\\
  & sum(\\{6,8,11\\}) = 25 \\\\
  & sum(\\{6,10,11\\}) = 27 \\\\
  & sum(\\{8,10,11\\}) = 29
\\end{align}$$

Baadhi ya jumla hizi hutokea zaidi ya mara moja, zingine ni za kipekee. Kwa seti $A$, acha $U(A,k)$ iwe seti ya jumla za kipekee za seti ndogo zenye vipengele $k$ vya $A$, katika mfano wetu tunapata $U(B,3) = \\{10,12,14,18,21,25,27,29\\}$ na $sum(U(B,3)) = 156$.

Sasa tafakari seti ya vipengele $100$ $S = \\{1^2, 2^2, \ldots , {100}^2\\}$. $S$ ina seti ndogo $100\,891\,344\,545\,564\,193\,334\,812\,497\,256\;$ zenye vipengele 50.

Tambua jumla ya nambari kamili zote ambazo ni jumla ya seti ndogo moja tu ya vipengele 50 za $S$, yaani pata $sum(U(S,50))$.
