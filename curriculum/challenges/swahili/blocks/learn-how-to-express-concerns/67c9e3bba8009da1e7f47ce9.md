# --explanation--

Maria anatumia **usemi ulioripotiwa** baada ya `they said` kuwasilisha kile timu ilichosema awali. Katika usemi ulioripotiwa, vitenzi mara nyingi hubadilika kuwa wakati uliopita, lakini `Present Continuous` (`are putting`) hubaki katika hali ya kuendelea (`they're already putting in extra hours`). Kwa mfano:

- Usemi wa moja kwa moja: `"We are working late," they said.`  

- Usemi ulioripotiwa: `They said they were working late.`

Hapa kuna mfano mwingine:

- Usemi wa moja kwa moja: `"I have finished the report," she said.`

- Usemi ulioripotiwa: `She said she had finished the report.`
