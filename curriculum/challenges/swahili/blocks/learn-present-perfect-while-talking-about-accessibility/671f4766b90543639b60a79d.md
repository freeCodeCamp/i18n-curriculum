# --explanation--

Aina hasi ya wakati wa `Present Perfect Continuous` huundwa kwa kutumia `hasn't/haven't + been + present participle`, ikionyesha tendo linaloendelea ambalo halijatokea kama ilivyotarajiwa kwa muda. Kwa mfano:

`He hasn't been exercising regularly.` - Hii ina maana ratiba yake ya mazoezi haikuwa ya kawaida.
  
Aina hasi ya wakati wa `Present Perfect` hutumia `hasn't/haven't + past participle`, ikielezea tendo ambalo halijakamilika hadi wakati huu. Kwa mfano:

`He hasn't exercised this week.` - Hii inaonyesha hakuna mazoezi yaliyofanyika wiki hii.
