### --question--

```html

</div>
```

---

```html

  <button role="tab" aria-select="true">Tab 1</button>
  <button role="tab" aria-select="false">Tab 2</button>
  <button role="tab" aria-select="false">Tab 3</button>
</div>
```

---

```html
<div role="tablist">
  <button role="tab" aria-selects="true">Tab 1</button>

</div>
```
