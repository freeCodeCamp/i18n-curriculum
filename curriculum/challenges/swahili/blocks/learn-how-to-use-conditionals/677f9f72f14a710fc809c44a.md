# --explanation--

Mark anataja `incompatible drivers` na `outdated software` kama sababu za kuanguka kwa programu.

`Incompatible drivers` ni dereva ambazo hazifanyi kazi vizuri na vifaa, wakati `outdated software` inamaanisha programu haijasasishwa hadi toleo lake la hivi karibuni. Vyote vinaweza kusababisha kuanguka. Kwa mfano:

- `The graphics card isn't working because of incompatible drivers.` - Dereva isiyo sahihi inasababisha kadi ya picha kushindwa kufanya kazi vizuri.

- `Outdated software can lead to security vulnerabilities.` - Programu za zamani huenda haziko salama kutumia kwa sababu ya ukosefu wa masasisho.
