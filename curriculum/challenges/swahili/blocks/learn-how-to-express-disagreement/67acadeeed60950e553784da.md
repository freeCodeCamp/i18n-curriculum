# --explanation--

Usemi `just as important` hutumika kulinganisha vitu viwili na kusisitiza kuwa vina umuhimu sawa. Huu unaonyesha kwamba hakuna kimoja kinachozidi kingine umuhimu. Kwa mfano:  

- `Exercise is just as important as eating healthy.` – Hii ina maana mazoezi na lishe bora vinachangia kwa usawa katika afya njema.  

- `Team communication is just as important as individual skills.` – Hii ina maana sababu zote mbili ni muhimu kwa usawa kwa mafanikio.

Anna hapingani na Bob bali anaongeza maoni yake. Kwa kusema `having the latest technology can be just as important`, anamaanisha kwamba teknolojia ni muhimu sawa na samani za ofisi linapokuja suala la uzalishaji. Usemi huu husaidia kusawazisha mawazo mawili bila kusema kwamba kimoja ni bora zaidi kuliko kingine.
