## --text--

哪一句正確使用了 `what`？

## --answers--

`She knows what does he like.`

### --feedback--

正確的結構應該是 `She knows what he likes`，語句中不應該有助動詞 `does`。

---

`What she is watching is very interesting?`

### --feedback--

這句話錯誤地以問號結尾，因為它不是問題而是述語。

---

`I don't understand what you mean.`

---

`Tell me what is your name.`

### --feedback--

正確的問題表單是 `What is your name?` 或 `Tell me what your name is.`
