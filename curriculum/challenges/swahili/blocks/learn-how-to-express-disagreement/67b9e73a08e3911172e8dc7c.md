# --explanation--

`However` hutumika kutambua mtazamo unaopingana kabla ya kuwasilisha hoja ya kupinga. Muundo huu hufanya mizozo kuwa na usawa zaidi na heshima katika mijadala. Kwa mfano:

- `The app is user-friendly. However, it still has some performance issues.` - Programu ni rahisi kutumia, lakini kuna matatizo ya kasi.

- `The software update improved security. However, it introduced a few new bugs.` - Sasisho lilifanya mfumo kuwa salama zaidi, lakini pia liliunda baadhi ya matatizo.

Anna kwanza anakubaliana na wasiwasi wa David kwa kusema `That’s a valid point`. Kisha, anatoa mtazamo tofauti kwa kutumia `however` kueleza kwamba zana za ushirikiano husaidia kutatua tatizo hilo.
