# --explanation--

`Trying to` inamaanisha kujitahidi kufanya jambo fulani. Kwa mfano:

`She's trying to finish the report before noon.` – Hii inaonyesha yeye anafanya kazi kwa bidii kufanya hilo.

`Monitor` ni kifaa kinachoonyesha picha na kinatumiwa sana pamoja na kompyuta. Kwa mfano:

`He connected a second monitor to his laptop.` – Hii inamaanisha aliongeza skrini nyingine.

`They've` ni kifupisho cha `they have`. Ni wakati wa Present Perfect unaoonyesha kitendo kilichofanyika kabla ya sasa au hali iliyotokea zamani na inaendelea.​ Kwa mfano:

`They've moved the files to a new folder.` – Hii inamaanisha mafaili yalihamishwa na wao.
