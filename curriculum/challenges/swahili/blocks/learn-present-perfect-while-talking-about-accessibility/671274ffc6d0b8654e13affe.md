# --explanation--

Wakati wa `Present Perfect Continuous` hutumika kuelezea matendo yaliyoanza zamani na bado yanaendelea. Unazingatia muda au kuendelea kwa tendo hilo. Muundo wa wakati huu ni `have/has + been + verb(-ing)`. Kwa mfano:

- `We have been developing a new app for accessibility.` - Hii ina maana ukuzaji ulianza zamani na bado unaendelea.

- `He has been fixing bugs in the software for several hours.` - Hii ina maana alianza kutatua hitilafu mapema na bado anafanya kazi juu yake.
