## --text--

$\cos\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)$의 반각 공식은 무엇인가요?

## --answers--

$\cos\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right) = \frac{1 + \cos(\theta)}{2}$

---

$\cos\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right) = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1 + \cos(\theta)}{2}}$

---

$\cos\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right) = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1 - \cos(\theta)}{2}}$

---

$\cos\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right) = \sqrt{1 - \cos^2(\theta)}$
