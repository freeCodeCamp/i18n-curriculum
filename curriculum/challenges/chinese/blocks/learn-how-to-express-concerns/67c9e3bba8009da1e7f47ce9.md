# --explanation--

Maria 在 `they said` 之后使用 **间接引语** 来分享团队之前的陈述。在间接引语中，动词通常变为过去式，但 `Present Continuous`（`are putting`）保持进行时态（`they're already putting in extra hours`）。例如：

- 直接引语：`"We are working late,"` 他们说。

- 转述：`They said they were working late.`

这是另一个示例：

- 直接引语：`"I have finished the report,"` 她说。

- 间接引语：`She said she had finished the report.`
