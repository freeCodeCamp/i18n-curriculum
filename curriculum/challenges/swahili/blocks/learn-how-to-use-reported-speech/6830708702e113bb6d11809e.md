# --explanation--

Sentensi hii inatumia **sauti ya pasipo** (passive voice), ambayo inalenga kwenye kitu kinachopokea tendo badala ya mtu anayetenda. Kwa mfano:  

`The navigation menu was appreciated.` – Hii ina maana watu walikipenda, lakini haijaeleza ni nani aliye kipenda.  

Katika **sauti ya hai** (active voice), ungesema `The users appreciated the navigation menu.` – Hii inaonyesha wazi ni nani aliyetenda.

**Sauti ya pasipo** hutumika mara nyingi wakati mtu anayetenda tendo hajulikani au si muhimu. Mfano mwingine:

`The report was completed on time.` – Unajua ripoti imetengenezwa, lakini hujui au huna haja ya kusema ni nani aliyeimaliza.
