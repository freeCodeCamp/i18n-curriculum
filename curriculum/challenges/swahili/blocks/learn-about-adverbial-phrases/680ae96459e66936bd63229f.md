# --explanation--

`What about...` hutumika kuuliza taarifa au kuanzisha mada mpya inayohusiana na kile kilichojadiliwa hivi karibuni. Mara nyingi hutumika kubadilisha makini au kuangalia kipengele kingine cha jambo fulani. Kwa mfano:

- `We finished the presentation. What about the handouts?` – Hii ina maana msemaji anauliza kama vipeperushi viko tayari au kama vimezingatiwa.

- `That plan sounds good. What about the timeline?` – Hii ina maana msemaji anakubaliana na wazo lakini anataka kuuliza ni muda gani utachukua.
