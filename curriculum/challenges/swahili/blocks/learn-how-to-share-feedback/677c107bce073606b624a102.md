`Thanks for joining the meeting this morning.` - Hii ina maana kwamba unashukuru kwa sababu mtu anahudhuria mkutano wa asubuhi.

`During the performance review, the manager highlighted her achievements over the past year.` - Hii ina maana kwamba meneja alitumia fursa wakati wa kagua kutaja mafanikio yake mwaka uliopita.
