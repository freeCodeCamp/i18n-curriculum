# --explanation--

`Installed` inamaanisha programu imewekwa na kutumika kwenye kifaa ili ifanye kazi ipasavyo. Kwa mfano:

`The installed program is working fine.` — Programu iliyowekwa inafanya kazi vizuri.

`By next day`, `by next week`, `by next month` zinamaanisha si baadaye kuliko mwisho wa siku/juma/mwezi ujao. Kwa mfano:

- `The update should be ready by next day.` — Sasisho litakamilika siku inayofuata.

- `We plan to finish testing by next month.` — Majaribio yanapaswa kufanyika kabla ya mwisho wa mwezi ujao.

Tom anasema sasisho hizi za programu ni za dharura na lazima zikamilishwe ndani ya muda maalum.
