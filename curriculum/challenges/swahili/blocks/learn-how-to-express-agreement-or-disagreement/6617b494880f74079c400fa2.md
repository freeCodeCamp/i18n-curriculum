# --description--

`Though` hutumika kuanzisha wazo linalopingana. Kwa mfano, `I like the plan, though I have some concerns` huanzisha tofauti na maoni chanya.

`Align` inamaanisha inalingana au inakubaliana na kitu. Kama vile, `Our strategy aligns with the company's goals` inamaanisha mkakati unakubaliana na au unafaa malengo.

# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inatumia `though` na `align` kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`I disagree, aligns with your idea.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii haitumii `though` kwa usahihi kuonyesha tofauti wala `aligns` kuonyesha makubaliano.

---

`The proposal is good, though it aligns with our objectives.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii haitumii `though` kwa usahihi. `Aligns` hapa ni sahihi lakini `though` inapaswa kuanzisha wazo linalopingana.

---

`The idea is interesting, though it doesn't quite align with our vision.`

---

`Everything aligns perfectly, though there are no issues.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii inapingana na yenyewe, kwani `though` inapaswa kuanzisha wazo linalopingana, si kukubaliana.
