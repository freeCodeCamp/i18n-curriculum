# --description--

Mstatili wa pande nne rahisi ni mstatili wa pande nne ambao una nukta nne tofauti, haina pembe za mstatili na haijavunjika yenyewe.

Acha $Q(m, n)$ iwe idadi ya mstatili wa pande nne rahisi ambao nukta zao ni nukta za kimiani zenye kuratibu ($x$, $y$) zinazokidhi $0 ≤ x ≤ m$ na $0 ≤ y ≤ n$.

Kwa mfano, $Q(2, 2) = 94$ kama inavyoonekana hapa chini:

Pia inaweza kuthibitishwa kuwa $Q(3, 7) = 39\\,590$, $Q(12, 3) = 309\\,000$ na $Q(123, 45) = 70\\,542\\,215\\,894\\,646$.

Tafuta $Q(12\\,345, 6\\,789)\bmod 135\\,707\\,531$.
