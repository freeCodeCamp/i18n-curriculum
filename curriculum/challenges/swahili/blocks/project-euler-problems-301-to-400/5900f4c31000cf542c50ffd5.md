# --description--

Chukulia nambari 50.

${50}^2 = 2500 = 2^2 × 5^4$, hivyo $φ(2500) = 2 × 4 × 5^3 = 8 × 5^3 = 2^3 × 5^3$. $φ$ inaashiria kitendakazi cha totient cha Euler.

Hivyo 2500 ni mraba na $φ(2500)$ ni kobe.

Tafuta jumla ya nambari zote $n$, $1 &lt; n &lt; {10}^{10}$ ambapo $φ(n^2)$ ni kobe.
