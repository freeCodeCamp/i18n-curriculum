# --explanation--

Swali `Have you thought` linatumia wakati wa `Present Perfect` kwa sababu linahusu tendo ambalo linaweza kuwa limetokea wakati wowote kabla ya sasa na bado lina umuhimu. Linatofautiana na `Did you think`, kwani hili la mwisho lingerejelea wakati maalum uliopita. Kwa mfano:  

- `Have you thought about learning a new language?` - Hili linauliza kama mtu amewahi kuzingatia jambo hilo wakati wowote kabla ya sasa.

- `Did you think about learning Japanese while you were in Japan?` - Hili linauliza kama mtu alitaka kuchukua kozi za Kijapani wakati alikuwa Japan (sasa hayuko huko).

`Additional` lina maana ya ziada au zaidi ya kitu fulani. Kwa mfano:

`We need additional resources to complete the project on time.` - Hii ina maana kwamba rasilimali zaidi zinahitajika.
