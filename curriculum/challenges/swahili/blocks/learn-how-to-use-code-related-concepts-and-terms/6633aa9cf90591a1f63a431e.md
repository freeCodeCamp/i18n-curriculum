# --description--

Kiunganishi `so` hutumika kuanzisha matokeo au athari inayohusiana na kile kilichosemwa. Mara nyingi huonyesha uhusiano wa sababu-na-athari kati ya sehemu mbili za sentensi.

Kwa mfano, `It was raining, so we canceled the picnic.` Hii inaonyesha kuwa picnic ilifutwa kwa sababu ya mvua.

# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia `so` kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`I was hungry so I am eating.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii karibu inatumia `so` kwa usahihi, lakini wakati katika sehemu ya pili unapaswa kuendana na wa kwanza. Itakuwa sahihi kusema: `I was hungry, so I ate.`

---

`She is late to work, so she misses the meeting.`

### --feedback--

Matumizi ya `so` yanapendekeza matokeo, lakini nyakati za vitenzi hazilingani. Inapaswa kuwa: `She is late to work, so she will miss the meeting.`

`It started raining, so we took shelter.`

`He likes to read books, so he buys a lamp.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii haitumii `so` kwa usahihi kwa sababu kununua taa si matokeo ya moja kwa moja ya kupenda kusoma vitabu. Matumizi bora yanaweza kuwa: `He likes to read at night, so he bought a lamp.`
