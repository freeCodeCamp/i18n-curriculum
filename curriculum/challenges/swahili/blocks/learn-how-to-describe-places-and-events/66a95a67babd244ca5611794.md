- `Have you finished your homework yet?` (Unatarajia kazi ya nyumbani kumalizika hivi karibuni, kama haijamalizika tayari).
- `Has the movie started yet?` (Unatarajia filamu kuanza hivi karibuni, kama haijaanza tayari).
