# --description--

`Is there` na `are there` hutumika mwanzoni mwa maswali kuuliza kuhusu kuwepo au uwepo wa kitu.

- `Is there` hutumika kwa nomino ya wingi mmoja, kama ilivyo katika `Is there a solution?`

- `Are there` hutumika kwa nomino ya wingi, kama ilivyo katika `Are there any questions?`

# --questions--

## --text--

Ni njia gani sahihi ya kuuliza kuhusu uwepo wa kitu kwa kutumia `there is` au `there are`?

## --answers--

`Is there a meeting scheduled for today?`

---

`Are there the new designer in the office?`

### --feedback--

Kumbuka kutumia `is there` kwa wingi mmoja na `are there` kwa wingi. Fomu sahihi ni `Is there a new designer in the office?`

---

`Is there any chairs in the meeting room?`

### --feedback--

`Chairs` ni wingi, kwa hivyo fomu sahihi ni `Are there any chairs in the meeting room?`

---

`Are there a new designer in the office?`

### --feedback--

Swali hili ni kuhusu mtu mmoja, kwa hivyo inapaswa kutumia `is there` badala ya `are there`. Fomu sahihi ni `Is there a new designer in the office?`
