# --description--

`They're`, `their`, na `there` zinafanana sauti lakini zina maana tofauti. `They're` ni kifupisho cha `they are`, `their` ni kitenzi cha umiliki cha `they`, na `there` inahusu mahali au huanzisha sentensi au kifungu.
