# --description--

Acha $C$ iwe mduara wenye mduara $r$, $x^2 + y^2 = r^2$. Tunachagua pointi mbili $P(a, b)$ na $Q(-a, c)$ ili mstari unaopita kupitia $P$ na $Q$ uwe mguso wa $C$.

Kwa mfano, nne-tuplet $(r, a, b, c) = (2, 6, 2, -7)$ inakidhi sifa hii.

Acha $F(R, X)$ iwe idadi ya nne-tuplet za nambari kamili $(r, a, b, c)$ zenye sifa hii, na zikiwa na $0 < r ≤ R$ na $0 < a ≤ X$.

Tunaweza kuthibitisha kuwa $F(1, 5) = 10$, $F(2, 10) = 52$ na $F(10, 100) = 3384$.

Tafuta $F({10}^8, {10}^9) + F({10}^9, {10}^8)$.
