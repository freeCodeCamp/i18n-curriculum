# --description--

`Too long` hutumika wakati jambo linachukua muda mrefu zaidi kuliko inavyohitajika au kutakiwa. Kwa mfano, `The meeting lasted four hours. Four hours is too long for a meeting!`

Hii ni tofauti na `so long`, ambayo mara nyingi hutumika kueleza kwamba jambo linachukua muda mwingi, lakini si lazima lifikie zaidi ya kile kinachotakiwa.
