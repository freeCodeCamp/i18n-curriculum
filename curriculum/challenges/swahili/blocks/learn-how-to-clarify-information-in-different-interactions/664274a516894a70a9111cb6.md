# --description--

`Now that I think about it` inamaanisha umekumbuka au kugundua kitu baada ya kufikiria zaidi. Kwa mfano, kama ulikuwa umesahau wapi uliweka funguo zako lakini kisha ukakumbuka, unaweza kusema, `Now that I think about it, they are on the table.` Inamaanisha umefikiria tena na kupata taarifa mpya.

`To confuse something with something else` inamaanisha kuchanganya vitu viwili na kufikiri kimoja ni kingine. Kwa mfano, kama unaona ndugu wa mrembo wa mtu na kufikiri ni rafiki yako, unaweza kusema `Sorry. I confused your twin with you`. Inamaanisha umechanganya kitu kimoja na kingine.
