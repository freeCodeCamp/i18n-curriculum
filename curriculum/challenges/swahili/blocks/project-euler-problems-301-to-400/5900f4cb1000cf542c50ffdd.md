# --description--

Orodha ya ukubwa $n$ ni mfululizo wa nambari asilia $n$. Mifano ni (2, 4, 6), (2, 6, 4), (10, 6, 15, 6), na (11).

Mgawanyiko mkubwa wa pamoja, au $gcd$, wa orodha ni nambari kubwa zaidi asilia inayogawanya maingizo yote ya orodha. Mifano: $gcd(2, 6, 4) = 2$, $gcd(10, 6, 15, 6) = 1$ na $gcd(11) = 11$.

Mara ndogo ya pamoja, au $lcm$, wa orodha ni nambari ndogo zaidi asilia inayogawanywa na kila ingizo la orodha. Mifano: $lcm(2, 6, 4) = 12$, $lcm(10, 6, 15, 6) = 30$ na $lcm(11) = 11$.

Acha $f(G, L, N)$ iwe idadi ya orodha za ukubwa $N$ zenye $gcd ≥ G$ na $lcm ≤ L$. Kwa mfano:

$$\begin{align}
  & f(10, 100, 1) = 91 \\\\
  & f(10, 100, 2) = 327 \\\\
  & f(10, 100, 3) = 1135 \\\\
  & f(10, 100, 1000)\bmod {101}^4 = 3\\,286\\,053
\end{align}$$

Tafuta $f({10}^6, {10}^{12}, {10}^{18})\bmod {101}^4$.
