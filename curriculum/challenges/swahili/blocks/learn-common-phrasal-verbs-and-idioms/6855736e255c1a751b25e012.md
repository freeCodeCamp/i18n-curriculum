# --explanation--

`Significant` ni kivumishi kinachomaanisha muhimu au chenye maana. `Significantly` ni kitenzi cha kivumishi hicho, na kinamaanisha kwa njia inayoonekana au muhimu. Kwa mfano:

- `That was a significant change in our design process.` – Hii ina maana mabadiliko yalikuwa muhimu.

- `The new feature significantly improved user satisfaction.` – Hii inaonyesha jinsi kipengele kilivyoathiri kuridhika kwa mtumizi – kwa njia yenye maana.

`To lead to something` ina maana ya kusababisha jambo fulani au kufanya liwezekane kutokea. Kwa mfano:

`Poor planning can lead to project delays.` – Hii ina maana mipango mibaya inaweza kusababisha kuchelewa.
