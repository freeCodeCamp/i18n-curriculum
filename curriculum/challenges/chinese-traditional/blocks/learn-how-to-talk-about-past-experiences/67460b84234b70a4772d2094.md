# --explanation--

`Absolutely` is used to express complete agreement or certainty. It is often used in informal or conversational contexts to emphasize strong agreement. For example: 

- **Zach:** `Do you think we should move forward with this plan?` 

- **Jessica:** `Absolutely!` - Here, `Absolutely` shows that the speaker is fully in favor of the plan and has no doubts.
