# --description--

`There is` na `there are` hutumika kuonyesha kuwepo au uwepo wa kitu. `There is` hutumika na nomino ya wingi mmoja au isiyohesabika, wakati `there are` hutumika na nomino za wingi.

Mifano:

- `There is a book on the table.` (Nomino ya wingi mmoja)

- `There is some water in the glass.` (Nomino isiyohesabika)

- `There are three apples in the basket.` (Nomino za wingi)
