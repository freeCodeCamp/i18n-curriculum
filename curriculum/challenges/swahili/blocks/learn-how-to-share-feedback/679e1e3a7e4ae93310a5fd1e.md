# --explanation--

`Several` lina maana ya zaidi ya machache lakini si lazima idadi kubwa. Linaonyesha kiasi kisichojulikana ambacho ni zaidi ya mbili. Kwa mfano:

`We've received several requests for a new feature.` - Hii ina maana kulikuwa na maombi mengi, lakini si idadi kubwa maalum.

Wakati wa `Present Perfect` katika `which has really helped the team` umetumika kwa sababu athari nzuri ya kugundua matatizo ilianza zamani na bado inaathiri timu sasa. Kwa mfano:

`Her comments in the software have made it easier for new employees to understand the code.` - Hii ina maana maoni yake bado yanasaidia leo.
