### --feedback--

# --explanation--

`Onboard` linamaanisha kusaidia wafanyakazi wapya kujifunza kazi yao, sheria za kampuni, na jinsi mifumo inavyofanya kazi. `Onboarding` ni nomino au fomu ya kuendelea. Kwa mfano:

- `The company onboards clients with a welcome guide.` - Kampuni huwapa wateja wapya taarifa kusaidia kuanza kutumia huduma.

- `Our onboarding process includes a training session.` - Watu wapya hupitia mafunzo wanapoanza.
