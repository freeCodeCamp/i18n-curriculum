# --explanation--

`Stick with` 意思是繼續堅持某個選擇或決定，而不是改變它。 例如：

- `I tried a new coding tool, but I'll stick with the one I know.`－你會繼續使用你熟悉的工具。

- `He wanted to switch to a different project, but he decided to stick with his current one.`－他選擇繼續在同一個專案工作。
