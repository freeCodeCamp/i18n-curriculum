## --text--

哪一句正確使用了 `since` 在 `Present Perfect Continuous` 時態？

## --answers--

`I have worked on the project since last week.`

### --feedback--

這是 `Present Perfect Simple` 時態。動詞形式應該是 `Present Perfect Continuous` 時態（`have been working`）以顯示持續進行的動作。

---

`I was testing the program since yesterday.`

### --feedback--

這是 `Past Simple` 時態。`Present Perfect Continuous` 時態應該使用 `have been` 而不是 `was`。

---

`I have been testing them since the latest software update.`

---

`I will have been working on the project since next month.`

### --feedback--

這裡不適合使用 `Future Perfect` 時態；`since` 用於 `Present Perfect Continuous` 時態。
