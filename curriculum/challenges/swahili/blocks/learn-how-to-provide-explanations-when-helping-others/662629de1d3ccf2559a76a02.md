# --description--

Kuanza `start` ni kuanzisha au kuanzisha jambo fulani. Kwa mfano, `you start a car by turning on the ignition`.

`Restart`, kwa upande mwingine, inamaanisha `start` kitu tena baada ya kusimama. Kwa mfano, `restarting a computer` inamaanisha kuzima kisha kuwasha tena ili kuonyesha upya mfumo wake.

# --questions--

## --text--

Tofauti gani kati ya `start` na `restart`?

## --answers--

`Start` inamaanisha kuendelea na jambo ambalo tayari linaendelea.

### --feedback--

`Start` kwa kweli inamaanisha kuanzisha jambo jipya.

---

`Start` inamaanisha kuanzisha jambo, na `restart` inamaanisha kuanzisha tena baada ya kusimama.

---

`Restart` inamaanisha kuendelea bila mapumziko yoyote.

### --feedback--

`Restart` inamaanisha kusimama kisha kuanza tena.

---

`Restart` inamaanisha kumaliza jambo kabisa.

### --feedback--

`Restart` inahusisha kuanza tena, si kumaliza.
