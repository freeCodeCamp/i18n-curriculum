# --description--

Wakati kitenzi kinapofuata kiambishi awali, mara nyingi huchukua fomu ya `-ing`, inayojulikana kama gerund. Gerund ni vitenzi vinavyotumika kama nomino. Kanuni hii ni ya kawaida katika misemo mingi ya Kiingereza. Kwa mfano:

- `After eating dinner, we watched a movie.` - Hapa, `eating` hufuata kiambishi awali `after`.

- `She is interested in learning Spanish.` - Hapa, `learning` hufuata kiambishi awali `in`.

- `Before going to bed, I like to read.` - Hapa, `going` hufuata kiambishi awali `before`.
