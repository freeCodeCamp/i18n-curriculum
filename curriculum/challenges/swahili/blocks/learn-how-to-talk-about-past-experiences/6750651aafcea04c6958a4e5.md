# --explanation--

Maneno kama `everything`, `something`, `anything`, na `nothing` ni viwakilishi visivyoeleweka ambavyo hutumia vitenzi vya umoja, ingawa vinaweza kumaanisha vitu vingi au dhana nyingi. Kwa mfano:  

- `Everything was ready for the presentation.` - `Everything` inahusu vitu vyote na inahitaji kitenzi cha umoja.

- `Something is wrong with the code.` - `Something` inahusu kitu kisichoeleweka na hutumia kitenzi cha umoja.

- `Is anything missing from the report?` - `Anything` inahusu kitu chochote kwa ujumla na hutumia kitenzi cha umoja.

- `Nothing was found in the logs.`  - `Nothing` inahusu ukosefu wa vitu na inahitaji kitenzi cha umoja.

Katika sentensi `Everything else was running smoothly`, unatumia `was` kwa sababu `everything` hutendewa kama umoja, ingawa inahusu vipengele vingi.
