# --description--

Wakati mtu anasema `I see where you're coming from`, ina maana anaelewa maoni au mtazamo wa mtu mwingine. Ni kama kusema, `I understand your thoughts or feelings.`

Kwa mfano, ikiwa rafiki anasema, `I don't like going out in the rain.` Na wewe unajibu, `I see where you're coming from.` Ina maana unaelewa kwa nini hawapendi, labda kwa sababu kupata unyevu si jambo zuri.

Katika mazingira ya kazi, kuelewa mahali mtu anapokuwa `coming from` husaidia katika mijadala. Ikiwa mwenzako anasema, `We should check our emails more often.` Na wewe unajibu, `I see where you're coming from.` Inaonyesha unaelewa sababu yao, kama vile kubaki na taarifa muhimu za hivi karibuni.
