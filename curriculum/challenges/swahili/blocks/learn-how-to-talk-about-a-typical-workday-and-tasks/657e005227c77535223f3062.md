# --description--

Unapozungumzia siku ya kawaida, hasa katika muktadha wa kitaalamu, mara nyingi hutumia wakati wa `present simple` kuelezea shughuli za kawaida au ratiba. Maneno kama `routine`, `typical`, na `workday` hutumika sana katika muktadha huu.

`Routine` inahusu njia ya kawaida ya kufanya mambo kwa mpangilio fulani. Kwa mfano, `My morning routine includes jogging and reading the news`.

`Typical` ina maana kwamba jambo ni la kawaida au la kawaida. Kwa mfano, `A typical day at work involves meetings and writing reports`.

`Workday` inahusu siku au saa unazofanya kazi. Kwa mfano, `My workday starts at 9 AM and ends at 5 PM`.
