# --explanation--

Unaweza kutumia wakati wa `Past Continuous` kuonyesha kwamba jambo lilikuwepo zamani. Kwa mfano:

- `She was thinking about the project all day yesterday.` - Hii inaonyesha kwamba kufikiri kulitokea kwa muda fulani zamani.

- `They were feeling nervous before the meeting.` - Hii inaonyesha hisia hiyo ilitokea kabla ya sasa na ilikuwa inaendelea wakati huo.

Katika sentensi, `I was worried about that`, Bob anaelezea wasiwasi wake kuhusu jambo lililotokea au lililokuwa linaendelea zamani.
