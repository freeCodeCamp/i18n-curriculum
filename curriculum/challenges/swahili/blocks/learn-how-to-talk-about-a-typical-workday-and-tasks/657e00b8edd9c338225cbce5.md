# --description--

Vitenzi vya wakati ni muhimu kuzungumzia lini jambo linatokea.

Kitenzi `in` kinatumika kwa miezi, miaka, karne, na vipindi virefu. Kwa mfano, `We will update the software in July`, `I started learning coding in 2022`, `I like to work in the morning`.

Kitenzi `on` kinatumika kwa siku na tarehe. Kwa mfano, `Our team meets on Mondays`, `The coding class is on April 10th`, `I finished the task on the last day`.

Kitenzi `at` kinatumika kubainisha nyakati halisi za mchana. Kwa mfano, `Our meeting is at 9 AM`, `I begin coding at 10 AM`, `We have lunch at 12 PM`.
