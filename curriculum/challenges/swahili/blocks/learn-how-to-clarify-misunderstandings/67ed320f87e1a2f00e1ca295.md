# --explanation--

`There's an issue with` ni njia ya kawaida ya kusema kuna tatizo linalohusiana na kitu fulani. Kwa mfano:

`There's an issue with the internet connection.` – Hii inamaanisha kuna tatizo na muunganisho.

`Software` inahusu programu au mifumo inayofanya kompyuta ifanye kazi, kama vile programu, mifumo ya uendeshaji, au zana. Kwa mfano:

`The software crashed when I tried to open the file.` – Hii inamaanisha programu ilisimama kufanya kazi ghafla ulipojaribu kufungua faili.
