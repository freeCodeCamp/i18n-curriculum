# --description--

Kitendakazi $lcm(a, b)$ kinaonyesha mzalishaji mdogo wa pamoja wa $a$ na $b$.

Acha $A(n)$ iwe wastani wa thamani za $lcm(n, i)$ kwa $1 ≤ i ≤ n$.

Mfano: $A(2) = \frac{2 + 2}{2} = 2$ na $A(10) = \frac{10 + 10 + 30 + 20 + 10 + 30 + 70 + 40 + 90 + 10}{10} = 32$.

Acha $S(n) = \sum A(k)$ kwa $1 ≤ k ≤ n$.

$S(100) = 122\\,726$.

Tafuta $S(99\\,999\\,999\\,019)\bmod 999\\,999\\,017$.
