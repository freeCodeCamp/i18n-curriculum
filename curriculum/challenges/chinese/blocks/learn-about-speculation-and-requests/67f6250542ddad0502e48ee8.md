## --text--

哪个句子语法正确？

## --answers--

`They might has missed the deadline because of a system error.`

### --feedback--

正确的形式是 `might have`，而不是 `might has`。

---

`They might missed the deadline due to a system error.`

### --feedback--

`might` 后面的动词应为动词原形。

---

`They might have missed the deadline because of a system error.`

---

`They might had missed the deadline if they weren't reminded.`

### --feedback--

在 `might` 之后，我们使用 `have`，而不是 `had`。

## --video-solution--

3

# --explanation--

情态动词 `might` 后接 `have` + `Past Participle` 用于表达过去的可能性。当你不确定某事是否发生，但认为有可能时使用此结构。

- 正确：`They might have missed the deadline.`

- 错误：`They might has missed...`、`They might missed...` 或 `They might had missed...`
