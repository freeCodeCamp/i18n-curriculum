# --explanation--

`Have to deal with` hutumika kuzungumzia jambo linalohitaji umakini au hatua. Lina maana kwamba kuna haja ya kushughulikia au kusimamia hali au tatizo fulani. Kwa mfano:

- `We have to deal with a lot of paperwork this week.` - Wanahitaji kushughulikia au kusimamia karatasi za kazi.

- `She has to deal with customer complaints every day.` - Kushughulikia malalamiko ni sehemu ya majukumu yake.

Katika swali, `What do we have to deal with now?`, Bob anauliza ni matatizo au majukumu gani yanayohitaji umakini wao kwa sasa.
