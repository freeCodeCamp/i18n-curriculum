# --explanation--

Unapotoa majibu mafupi kwa swali lililokuulizwa kuhusu jambo ulilofanya au usilofanya zamani, tumia `Yes, I did.` au `No, I didn't` kujibu kwa kifupi. Kwa mfano:

- `Did you buy that car you wanted? No, I didn't.` - Hii ina maana hukununua gari.

- `Did you pass the English test? Yes, I did` - Hii ina maana ulipita mtihani.
