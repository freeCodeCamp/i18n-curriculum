## --text--

Which option means "I'm Chinese. I'm a developer."?

## --answers--

`我是開發者 (wǒ shì kāi fā zhě)，我是中國人 (wǒ shì zhōng guó rén)。`

### --feedback--

This says "I'm a developer. I'm Chinese.", which is not the order given in the question.

---

`我是中國 (wǒ shì zhōng guó)，我是開發者 (wǒ shì kāi fā zhě)。`

### --feedback--

`我是中國 (wǒ shì zhōng guó)` means "I am China", which is not the way to express nationality.

---

`我是中國人 (wǒ shì zhōng guó rén)，我是開發者 (wǒ shì kāi fā zhě)。`

---

`我是中國人 (wǒ shì zhōng guó rén)，我是設計師 (wǒ shì shè jì shī)。`

### --feedback--

`設計師 (shè jì shī)` means "designer", not "developer".

# --explanation--

`我是中國人 (wǒ shì zhōng guó rén)` means "I am Chinese". `我是開發者 (wǒ shì kāi fā zhě)` means "I am a developer". These are introductions of nationality and profession.
