`I can accessing my account because I forgot my password.`

# --explanation--

Ili kuunda kukanusha kwa kitenzi cha modal, unahitaji tu kuongeza `not` baada ya kitenzi cha modal. Kwa mfano:

- `You shouldn't ignore security updates on your computer.` - Ni muhimu kusakinisha masasisho ya usalama, kwa hivyo usiyapuuze.

- `We might not have enough time to finish the project by the deadline.` - Kuna uwezekano kwamba hawatamaliza mradi kwa wakati.

Kwa modals `can` na `should`, fomu ya kukanusha kawaida hupunguzwa (`can't` na `shouldn't`).
