`We fixed the login error.` – Tatizo la kuingia sasa limepatiwa suluhisho.

`Many of the bugs were found during testing.` – Idadi kubwa ya hitilafu (si zote) ziligunduliwa.

`From before` inahusu kitu kilichotokea zamani. Kwa mfano:  

`I remember this issue from before.` – Ni kitu kilichotokea awali kwa wakati.
