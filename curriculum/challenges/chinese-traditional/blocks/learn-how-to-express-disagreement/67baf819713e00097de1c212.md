# --explanation--

`Team-building activities` 描述旨在加強團隊合作與協作的練習。舉例來說：

- `Outdoor team-building activities like hiking or obstacle courses strengthen teamwork。` - 實體的挑戰鼓勵合作與聯繫。

- `Cooking classes are unique team-building activities that encourage collaboration and creativity。`－一起準備食物是增強團隊精神的有趣方式。

`Virtual team-building activities` 是為了幫助遠端團隊而設計的。 例如：

- `Virtual team-building activities like icebreaker questions help new employees feel welcome。`－提出有趣的問題讓新進員工更容易與團隊建立連結。

- `Online escape rooms are great virtual team-building activities for problem-solving。`－團隊在虛擬環境中共同合作解謎。
