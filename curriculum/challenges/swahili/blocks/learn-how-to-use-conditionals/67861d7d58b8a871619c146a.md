# --explanation--

Usemi `doesn't look likely` una maana kwamba jambo linaonekana lisitokee au lisilo la kawaida kutokea. Kwa mfano:

- `It doesn't look likely that we'll finish the project on time.` - Inaonekana lisitokee kufikia tarehe ya mwisho.

- `With this weather, it doesn't look likely that we'll have a picnic today.` - Mvua au hali mbaya ya hewa hufanya picnic ionekane lisitokee.
