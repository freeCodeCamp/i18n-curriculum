# --description--

`One of these days` es una frase que usas cuando hablas de algo que planeas hacer pronto, pero no has decidido exactamente cuándo. It's like saying "sometime soon" or "in the near future". For example:

`One of these days, I'll visit Paris.` - This means you want to visit Paris and hope to do it sometime soon, but you don't know the exact day yet.
