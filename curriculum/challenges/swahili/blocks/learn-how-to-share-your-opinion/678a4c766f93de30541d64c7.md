# --questions--

## --text--

Ni sentensi gani inayotumia neno `prefer` kwa usahihi?

## --answers--

`I prefer to go shopping yesterday.`
  
### --feedback--

Sentensi hii iko katika wakati usio sahihi kwa ajili ya kuelezea upendeleo kuhusu shughuli za zamani.

---

`She prefers drink coffee every morning.`

### --feedback--

Baada ya `prefer`, unahitaji aina ya `-ing` ya kitenzi.

---

`He prefers running in the park on weekends.`

---

`They prefer to play soccer on weekends.`

### --feedback--

Sentensi hii inatumia aina ya infinitive `to play` baada ya `prefer`. Aina sahihi hapa ni `-ing`.

3

# --explanation--

`Prefer` kawaida huambatana na nomino au aina ya `-ing` ya kitenzi. Kwa mfano:

- `Users often prefer apps with simple and clean designs.` - Watu kawaida huchagua programu ambazo ni rahisi kuvinjari na zina mvuto wa kuona.

- `Some people prefer using dark mode to reduce eye strain.` - Watumizi wengi huchagua hali ya giza kwa sababu ni rahisi kwa macho yao.
