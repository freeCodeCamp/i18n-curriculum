## --text--

哪一句語法正確？

## --answers--

`They might has missed the deadline because of a system error.`

### --feedback--

正確的表單是 `might have`，而不是 `might has`。

---

`They might missed the deadline due to a system error.`

### --feedback--

`might` 後面的動詞應該是動詞的原形。

---

`They might have missed the deadline because of a system error.`

---

`They might had missed the deadline if they weren't reminded.`

### --feedback--

在 `might` 之後，我們使用 `have`，而不是 `had`。

## --video-solution--

3

# --explanation--

情態動詞 `might` 後面接 `have` + `Past Participle` 用來表達過去的可能性。當你不確定某事是否發生，但認為有可能時，會使用這個結構。

- 正確：`They might have missed the deadline.`

- 錯誤：`They might has missed...`、`They might missed...` 或 `They might had missed...`
