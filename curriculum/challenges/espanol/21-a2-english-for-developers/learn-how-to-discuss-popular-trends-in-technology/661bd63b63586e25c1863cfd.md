# --description--

`For` es una preposición que indica el propósito, objetivo o razón de una acción. A menudo le dice a las personas por qué algo está sucediendo o para quién está destinado. Examples: `She studied hard for the exam.` (the purpose of studying was the exam). `They bought a gift for their friend's birthday.` (the reason for buying it was the friend's birthday).
