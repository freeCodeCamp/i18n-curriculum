# --description--

Vitenzi vya awali ni maneno yanayounganisha majina, viwakilishi, au misemo na maneno mengine ndani ya sentensi na mara nyingi huonyesha wakati, mahali, au uhusiano, kama `with`, `for`, `about`. Pia vinaweza kuonekana mwishoni mwa swali au sentensi, jambo ambalo ni la kawaida katika Kiingereza cha mdomo.

Mifano:

Wakati mtu anauliza `who are you going with?` preposition `with` hutumika mwishoni kuuliza kuhusu kampuni atakayokuwa nayo.

`What are you excited about?` Katika swali hili, `about` hutumika mwishoni kuuliza kuhusu somo au tukio linalosababisha msisimko.

`Who are you waiting for?` Hapa, `for` imewekwa mwishoni mwa swali kuuliza kuhusu mtu anayemsubiri mtu mwingine.

`What are you speaking of?`

### --feedback--

Ingawa `of` ni preposition, sentensi hii inasikika kidogo isiyo ya kawaida au rasmi. Itakuwa ya kawaida zaidi kuuliza, `What are you talking about?`

`Where is it belong?`

### --feedback--

Sentensi sahihi inapaswa kuwa `Where does it belong?` bila preposition mwishoni, kwani `belong` kawaida haitaji preposition.

---

`Who do you want to meet with she?`

### --feedback--

Fomu sahihi inapaswa kuwa `Who do you want to meet with?` `With she` si sahihi kisarufi; inapaswa kuwa `with her`.
