# --explanation--

Jibu sahihi, `I read they might be having money problems`, linajibu moja kwa moja swali la Tom, `Why do you ask?` Linatoa sababu wazi ya kuanzisha mada ya TechCorp huenda ikafungwa.

Wakati mtu anauliza `Why do you ask?`, wanatafuta maelezo maalum au muktadha wa swali lako.

Mifano mingine ya majibu kwa `Why do you ask?` inaweza kuwa:

- `I saw something about it in the news.`

- `I heard someone mention it in a meeting.`

Majibu haya yanatoa muktadha unaohitajika na kuendelea na mazungumzo kwa makini na swali la awali.
