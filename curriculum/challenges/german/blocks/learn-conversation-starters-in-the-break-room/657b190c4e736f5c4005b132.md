# --description--

`To be into...` means to be interested in something or to enjoy it.

`These` is a demonstrative pronoun that refers to specific items or ideas mentioned previously in the conversation.

When Sarah asks, `Are you into these activities?`, she uses `these` to refer back to something Tom just mentioned. Es ist ein Weg, um genau zu spezifizieren, über welche Aktivitäten sie redet, ohne den ganzen Satz zu wiederholen.
