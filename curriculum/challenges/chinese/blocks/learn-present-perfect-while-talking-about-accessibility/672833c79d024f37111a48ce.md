# --questions--

## --text--

哪个句子正确使用了 `since` 在 `Present Perfect Continuous` 时态中？

## --answers--

`I have worked on the project since last week.`

### --feedback--

这是 `Present Perfect Simple` 时态。动词形式应为 `Present Perfect Continuous` 时态（`have been working`），以表示正在进行的动作。

---

`I was testing the program since yesterday.`

### --feedback--

这是 `Past Simple` 时态。`Present Perfect Continuous` 时态应使用 `have been` 而不是 `was`。

---

`I have been testing them since the latest software update.`

---

`I will have been working on the project since next month.`

### --feedback--

这里不适合使用 `Future Perfect` 时态；`since` 用于 `Present Perfect Continuous` 时态。
