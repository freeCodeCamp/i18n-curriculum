# --explanation--

`Quite` ni **kiongezaji nguvu** kinachomaanisha "badala ya" au "sana," kulingana na muktadha. Kinatumika kusisitiza kivumishi kinachofuata. Kwa mfano:

- `The task is quite difficult.` - Hii inamaanisha ni vigumu sana au badala ya vigumu, kulingana na msisitizo.

- `She was quite tired after the long day.` - Hii inamaanisha alikuwa amechoka sana, lakini si kabisa kuchoka kabisa.
