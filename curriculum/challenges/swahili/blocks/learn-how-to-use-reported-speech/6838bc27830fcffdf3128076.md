# --explanation--

`Exactly` ni kitenzi kinachotumika kuonyesha makubaliano kamili au usahihi na kile mtu mwingine amesema. `Exact`, kwa upande mwingine, ni kivumishi kinachomaanisha sahihi au kamili kwa undani. Kwa mfano:

- `You are exactly right about the cause of the problem.` – Hii inaonyesha makubaliano kamili. Inaonyesha jinsi mtu alivyokuwa sahihi (kivumishi).

- `We need the exact measurements to build the new server rack.` – Hii inamaanisha vipimo sahihi sana. Inahusiana na nomino badala ya kitenzi au kivumishi.
