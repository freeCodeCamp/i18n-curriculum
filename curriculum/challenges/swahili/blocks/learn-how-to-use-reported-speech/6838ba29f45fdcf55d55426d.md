# --explanation--

`Perhaps` ina maana ya labda au pengine, ikionyesha kwamba jambo fulani halijulikani kwa uhakika lakini lina uwezekano. Kwa mfano:

`Perhaps we should check the logs again.` – Hii inaonyesha wazo bila uhakika.

`Had` + subject + verb in the `past participle` ni mbadala wa `If` + subject + `had` + verb in the `past participle` na hutumika katika sentensi za `Third Conditional` kuzungumzia hali za dhana za zamani. Inasisitiza hali ambayo haikutokea lakini ingeweza kubadilisha matokeo. Kwa mfano:

`Had we fixed the bug earlier, the system wouldn't have crashed.` - Hii ina maana sawa na `If we had fixed the bug earlier, the system wouldn't have crashed.`
