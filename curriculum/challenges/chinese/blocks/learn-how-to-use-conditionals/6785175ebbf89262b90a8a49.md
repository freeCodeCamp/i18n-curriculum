## --text--

哪个句子语法正确？

## --answers--

`If that happened, a lot of smaller companies were be in trouble, too.`

### --feedback--

对于条件式，如果条件使用 `Past Simple` 时态（假设），结果应使用 `would`。

---

`If that happened, a lot of smaller companies would be in trouble, too.`

---

`If that happened, a lot of smaller companies will be in trouble, too.`

### --feedback--

在 `Second Conditional` 中使用 `will` 是错误的。假设条件的结果应使用 `would`。

---

`If that happens, a lot of smaller companies will be in trouble, too.`

### --feedback--

这个句子使用了 `First Conditional`，它用于真实可能性。然而，给出的句子是一个假设情景，因此是 `Second Conditional`。

# --explanation--

正确的句子，`If that happened, a lot of smaller companies would be in trouble, too`，是一个 `Second Conditional`，用于表示现在或将来的假设或不真实情况。在这个句子中：

- `If that happened` 是假设条件。

- `A lot of smaller companies would be in trouble, too` 是想象中的结果。
