# --explanation--

`Don't you think?` inaweza kutumika kuuliza maoni ya mtu baada ya kushiriki naye mawazo yako.

Kusema kwamba kile ulichoambia kina mantiki, wanaweza kujibu `I understand`, ambayo si lazima ionyeshe makubaliano, lakini inaonyesha kwamba wanadhani kile ulichoambia ni cha kimantiki. Kwa mfano:

- **Kevin:** `Studying is better than not doing anything, don't you think?`

- **Oliver:** `I understand, but...` - Hii ina maana mtu anakubali mantiki nyuma ya kile ulichoambia. Kutumia `but` mara moja baadae kunaonyesha kwamba wanaweza kusema kitu kinachopingana na wazo hili la awali.
