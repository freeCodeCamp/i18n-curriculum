# --explanation--

Unapojiuliza swali linalojumuisha swali lingine ndani yake, swali la pili hufuata muundo wa sentensi ya kawaida (mhusika + kitenzi), si muundo wa swali. Kwa mfano:

`Do you know where he put the report?` - Swali `where did he put the report?` linafuata swali lingine, `Do you know...`, kwa hivyo unapaswa kutumia muundo wa sentensi chanya pamoja nalo. `Do you know where did he put the report?`, kwa hiyo, ingekuwa si sahihi.

Hii hutokea kwa sababu `Do you know` tayari inafanya sentensi kuwa swali. Sehemu ya pili, `where he put the report`, ni taarifa ambayo mtu anataka kujua, hivyo hufuata mpangilio wa kawaida wa mhusika na kitenzi wa sentensi chanya.

Linganisho na swali la moja kwa moja:

`Where did he put the report?`

Hapa, `Where` huanzisha swali, kwa hivyo unahitaji muundo wa kawaida wa swali (`did he put`).

Mfano mwingine:

`Did she explain why the meeting was canceled?` - Hapa, swali ni `Did she explain` na `why the meeting was canceled` ni taarifa aliyoeleza. `Did she explain why was the meeting canceled?` ingekuwa si sahihi.

Kama kanuni ya jumla, ikiwa sentensi inaanza na neno la swali (`Where`, `Why`, `How` nk), tumia mpangilio wa neno la swali, kama ilivyo `Where did he go?`. Ikiwa neno la swali linakuja baada ya swali lingine, tumia mpangilio wa kawaida wa maneno, kama ilivyo `Do you know where he went?`.
