# --description--

Nambari kamili huitwa B-laini ikiwa hakuna gawanyio msingi lake lolote linalozidi $B$.

Acha $SB(n)$ iwe gawanyio kubwa zaidi la B-laini la $n$.

Mifano:

$$\begin{align}
  & S_1(10) = 1 \\\\
  & S_4(2\\,100) = 12 \\\\
  & S_{17}(2\\,496\\,144) = 5\\,712
\end{align}$$

Fafanua $F(n) = \displaystyle\sum_{B = 1}^n \sum_{r = 0}^n S_B(\displaystyle\binom{n}{r})$. Hapa, $\displaystyle\binom{n}{r}$ inaashiria nambari ya binomial.

Mifano:

$$\begin{align}
  & F(11) = 3132 \\\\
  & F(1\\,111)\bmod 1\\,000\\,000\\,993 = 706\\,036\\,312 \\\\
  & F(111\\,111)\bmod 1\\,000\\,000\\,993 = 22\\,156\\,169
\end{align}$$

Tafuta $F(11\\,111\\,111)\bmod 1\\,000\\,000\\,993$.
