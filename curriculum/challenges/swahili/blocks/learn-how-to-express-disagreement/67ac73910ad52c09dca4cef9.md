# --explanation--

`Not necessarily` ni njia ya heshima ya dhihirisha kutokubaliana kidogo. Badala ya kusema moja kwa moja `no`, inapendekeza kwamba kuna mambo mengine ya kuzingatia. Katika mazungumzo, msemo huu husaidia kuweka mijadala wazi na yenye heshima. Kwa mfano:

- **Sarah:** `I think upgrading the computer's processor will fix the issue.`

- **Finn:** `Not necessarily. The problem could be with the software, not the hardware.` - Mtu huyu anapendekeza kwamba kuboresha processor huenda hakutatatua tatizo kwa sababu linaweza kusababishwa na jambo lingine, kama programu za zamani.
