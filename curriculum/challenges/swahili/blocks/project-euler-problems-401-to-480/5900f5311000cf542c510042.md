# --description--

Chukulia nambari 15.

Kuna nambari nane chanya chini ya 15 ambazo ni coprime na 15: 1, 2, 4, 7, 8, 11, 13, 14.

Inversi za modulo za nambari hizi modulo 15 ni: 1, 8, 4, 13, 2, 11, 7, 14 kwa sababu

$$\begin{align}
  & 1  \times 1\bmod 15 = 1 \\\\
  & 2  \times 8  = 16\bmod 15 = 1 \\\\
  & 4  \times 4  = 16\bmod 15 = 1 \\\\
  & 7  \times 13 = 91\bmod 15 = 1 \\\\
  & 11 \times 11 = 121\bmod 15 = 1 \\\\
  & 14 \times 14 = 196\bmod 15 = 1
\end{align}$$

Acha $I(n)$ iwe nambari chanya kubwa zaidi $m$ ndogo kuliko $n - 1$ ambapo inversi ya modulo ya $m$ modulo $n$ ni sawa na $m$ yenyewe.

Hivyo $I(15) = 11$.

Pia $I(100) = 51$ na $I(7) = 1$.

Tafuta $\sum I(n)$ kwa $3 ≤ n ≤ 2 \times {10}^7$
