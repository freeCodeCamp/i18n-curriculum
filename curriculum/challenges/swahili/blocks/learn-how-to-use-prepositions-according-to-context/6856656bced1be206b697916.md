# --explanation--

`We hired a short-term contractor to help this week.` – Hii ina maana msaada ni wa muda tu.

`We worked remotely during the summer.` – Hii ina maana katika msimu huo mzima.

`The trial period lasts two weeks.` – Hii ina maana kipindi cha wiki mbili cha kujaribu jambo.
