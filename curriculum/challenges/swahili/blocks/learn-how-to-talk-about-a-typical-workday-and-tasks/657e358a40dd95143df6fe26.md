# --description--

Kuelewa maswali kuhusu uzoefu wa kazi kunahusisha kujua misemo muhimu na maana yake. `How's` ni kifupisho cha `how is`, kinachotumika sana katika mazungumzo ya kawaida. Kwa mfano, `How's your day?` ni sawa na `How is your day?`.

`Security` inahusu mazoea ya kuweka watu na maeneo salama. Katika sentensi, unaweza kusema, `He works in security, protecting buildings`.

Kusema `deal with` kunamaanisha kushughulikia au kusimamia jambo fulani, hasa changamoto. Kwa mfano, `She deals with client requests at her job`.

`Danger` inahusu uwezekano wa madhara au hatari. Sentensi ya mfano inaweza kuwa, `Firefighters often face danger in their work`.
