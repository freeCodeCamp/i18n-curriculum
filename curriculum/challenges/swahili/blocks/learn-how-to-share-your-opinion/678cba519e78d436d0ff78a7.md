# --explanation--

`I'd` ni kifupisho cha `I would`, kinachotumika kuzungumzia upendeleo binafsi. Kwa mfano:

`I'd like to finish the project by the end of the week.` - Kinaonyesha matakwa yako au upendeleo wa kumaliza mradi.

`Rather` hutumika kuonyesha upendeleo kati ya chaguzi mbili. Mara nyingi huambatana na `would` katika misemo kama `I would rather`. Kwa mfano:

`I would rather use Python for data analysis than JavaScript.` - Kinaonyesha chaguo lako la kitu kimoja juu ya kingine.
