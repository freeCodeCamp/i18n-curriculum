`I'm booking a meeting room for the team.` – Hii ina maana kuwa uhifadhi umefanywa kwa ajili ya matumizi yao.

`He stepped in while the manager was away.` – Hii ina maana alijaza nafasi hiyo kwa muda mfupi.
